## FAQ

**What is the most important GEO statistic in 2026?**

AI traffic converts at 14.2% compared to 2.8% for Google organic. That 5x conversion advantage means fewer total visits but significantly more revenue per visitor. GEO is not about traffic volume. It is about traffic quality.

**How often are these GEO statistics updated?**

We update this page quarterly. Last updated: April 2026. All statistics include their source and publication year.

**Where do these statistics come from?**

All statistics come from named, verifiable sources including Superlines, Semrush, Ahrefs, First Page Sage, Gartner, Incremys, and academic research. Each stat includes a direct source link.

**What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?**

GEO is the practice of optimizing content so AI search engines cite and reference it. Unlike traditional SEO (which targets Google rankings), GEO targets visibility in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and other AI platforms. Learn more in our [generative engine optimization guide](/blog/generative-engine-optimization-guide).

**Is GEO replacing traditional SEO?**

No. GEO complements SEO. 76.1% of URLs cited in AI Overviews also rank in Google's top 10. Strong traditional SEO remains the foundation. GEO adds a new visibility layer on top.

**How does AI citation differ from traditional search ranking?**

80% of URLs cited by AI platforms do not rank in Google's top 100. AI uses different signals: content structure, freshness, statistics, author authority, and readability. Traditional ranking factors like backlinks matter less for AI citation.
