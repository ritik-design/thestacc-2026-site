## FAQ

**How many social media posts should a business publish per week?**

3 to 5 posts per week on your primary platforms. The ideal cadence depends on the platform: 4 to 7 on Instagram (including Stories), 3 to 5 on Facebook, 2 to 3 on LinkedIn, and 3 to 5 on TikTok. Consistency matters more than volume. 3 posts per week for 12 months outperforms 10 posts per week for 2 months.

**What type of social media content gets the most engagement?**

Short-form video (Reels, TikTok) generates the highest reach. Carousel posts drive the highest engagement rate on Instagram at [1.92% average](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks/instagram). Interactive content (polls, questions, quizzes) drives comments. And customer testimonials build the most trust. Mix all 4 types for a balanced strategy.

**How do I come up with social media content ideas every day?**

Use a categorized list like this one. Pick 1 idea from a different category each day. Batch your content creation weekly. And recycle your best-performing posts every 60 to 90 days. Most audiences do not remember a post you shared 3 months ago.

**What is the 80/20 rule for social media content?**

80% of your content should educate, entertain, or build trust. 20% should directly promote your products or services. Flipping this ratio turns your feed into a billboard that followers unfollow. Value-first content builds the audience. Promotional content converts them.

**How far in advance should I plan social media content?**

Plan 2 to 4 weeks ahead. Use a [content calendar](/blog/create-content-calendar-seo) to map posts by category and platform. Leave room for trending topics and real-time content. Batch creation on one day per week keeps the schedule manageable.
