**How much do businesses spend on video marketing?**

Global digital video ad spending is projected at $223.5 billion in 2026. At the individual level, 42% of video marketers spend $0-$500 per video. AI tools have reduced production costs by 91%, making video accessible at nearly any budget.

**Does video help with SEO?**

Yes. Websites with video are 53x more likely to rank on Google's first page. Video drives a 157% increase in organic traffic. Videos appear in 62% of Google searches worldwide. YouTube videos with transcripts rank higher for 95% of their target keywords.

**How is AI changing video marketing?**

75% of video marketers use AI tools. AI reduced production costs by 91% and cut production time from 13 days to 27 minutes for a 60-second video. AI video generation volume grew 840% in 2 years. 96% of marketers believe AI will be central to video marketing.
