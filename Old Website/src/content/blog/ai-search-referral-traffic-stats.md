## Overall AI Referral Traffic Growth

**1. AI-referred sessions grew 527% in 5 months (January to May 2025).** Sessions jumped from 17,076 to 107,100 across 400+ websites and 12 industries. (Source: [Superprompt / Previsible AI Traffic Report](https://superprompt.com/blog/ai-traffic-up-527-percent-how-to-get-cited-by-chatgpt-claude-perplexity-2025), 2025)

**2. AI platforms generated 1.13 billion referral visits in June 2025.** That represents a 357% increase from June 2024. (Source: [Digiday](https://digiday.com/media/in-graphic-detail-the-state-of-ai-referral-traffic-in-2025/), 2025)

**3. AI referral traffic now accounts for 1.08% of all website traffic.** It grows approximately 1 percentage point month over month. (Source: [Conductor](https://www.asklantern.com/blogs/chatgpt-drives-87-of-ai-referral-traffic), 2026)

**4. AI referral traffic grew 700% in 2025.** Full-year growth outpaced most other traffic sources by orders of magnitude. (Source: [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/ai-traffic-up-seo-rewritten-459954), 2025)

**5. Gen AI traffic is growing 165x faster than organic search traffic.** Organic still dominates in volume. AI dominates in growth velocity. (Source: [Superlines](https://www.superlines.io/articles/ai-search-statistics/), 2026)

**6. Traffic to the top 10 AI chatbots grew 81% year over year in 2025.** Total visits reached 55.2 billion. (Source: [Similarweb via Position Digital](https://www.position.digital/blog/ai-seo-statistics/), 2025)

**7. AI search tools captured 12% to 15% of global search market share by end of 2025.** Up from 5% to 6% at the start of the year. (Source: [ALM Corp](https://almcorp.com/blog/ai-search-trends/), 2025)

## AI Overviews and Zero-Click Impact

**22. Google AI Overviews appear on 48% of all search queries as of March 2026.** Up from 13.14% in March 2025 and 34.5% in December 2025. (Source: [Stackmatix](https://www.stackmatix.com/blog/google-ai-overview-seo-impact), 2026)

**23. AI Overviews reduce organic CTR by 61%.** Click-through rates dropped from 1.76% to 0.61% for queries showing AI Overviews. (Source: [Seer Interactive](https://www.seerinteractive.com/insights/aio-impact-on-google-ctr-september-2025-update), 2025)

**24. 93% of AI search sessions end without a website click.** Only 7% of sessions result in a referral to an external site. (Source: [Semrush](https://www.superlines.io/articles/ai-search-statistics/), 2025)

**25. Zero-click searches reached 58.5% of all US searches in 2025.** That rate rises to 83% when AI Overviews are present. (Source: [The Digital Bloom](https://thedigitalbloom.com/learn/2025-organic-traffic-crisis-analysis-report/), 2025)

**26. Brands cited in AI Overviews earn 35% more organic clicks.** They also earn 91% more paid clicks. Being cited offsets the CTR drop. (Source: [Search Engine Journal](https://www.searchenginejournal.com/impact-of-ai-overviews-how-publishers-need-to-adapt/556843/), 2026)

**27. Organic click share dropped 11 to 23 percentage points across every vertical.** This decline accelerated in Q1 2026. (Source: [The Digital Bloom](https://thedigitalbloom.com/learn/organic-traffic-crisis-report-2026-update/), 2026)

**28. 75% of Google AI Mode sessions end without external site visits.** AI Mode is even more zero-click than standard AI Overviews. (Source: [Superlines](https://www.superlines.io/articles/ai-search-statistics/), 2026)

The zero-click trend is real. But websites cited in AI responses gain traffic while others lose it. Learn how to [optimize for Google AI Overviews](/blog/optimize-google-ai-overviews) and [reduce the impact of zero-click searches](/blog/zero-click-search-seo).

## FAQ

**How much has AI search referral traffic grown?**

AI referral sessions grew 527% between January and May 2025. Full-year 2025 growth reached 700%. AI platforms generated 1.13 billion referral visits in June 2025 alone, a 357% increase from June 2024.

**Which AI platform sends the most referral traffic?**

ChatGPT drives 87.4% of all AI referral traffic. Perplexity is second at 15%. Google Gemini is the fastest growing at 388% growth in late 2025. Microsoft Copilot grew 25.2x in the same period.

**Does AI referral traffic convert better than organic search?**

Yes. Multiple studies show AI traffic converts at 4.4x to 23x the rate of organic search. ChatGPT ecommerce traffic converts 31% higher than non-branded organic. AI-referred visitors spend 68% more time on sites.

**How do Google AI Overviews affect organic traffic?**

AI Overviews appear on 48% of Google searches as of March 2026. They reduce organic CTR by 61%. But brands cited in AI Overviews earn 35% more organic clicks than uncited brands. Getting cited is now critical.

**What industries get the most AI referral traffic?**

IT leads at 2.8% of total traffic from AI. Legal (1.34%), finance (1.21%), and healthcare (0.89%) follow. Legal saw the fastest growth at 823% year over year. Consumer electronics, travel, and financial services show 40% to 55% AI-influenced purchase rates.
