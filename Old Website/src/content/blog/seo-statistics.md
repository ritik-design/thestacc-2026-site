## Organic Search and Click Statistics

**9. 68% of online experiences begin with a search engine.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025. Organic search remains the primary entry point for web users.

**10. Organic search drives 53.3% of all website traffic.**
Source: [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/organic-search-traffic-down-yoy-data-467748), 2025

**11. 94% of web pages receive zero traffic from Google.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025. Only 6% of published pages generate any [organic traffic](/glossary/organic-traffic) at all.

**12. The #1 organic result on Google gets a 39.8% click-through rate.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025

**13. 68.7% of all clicks go to the top 3 organic results.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025. If you are not in the top 3, you are splitting the remaining 31% with every other result.

**14. Only 0.78% of users click on second-page results.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025. Page 2 of Google is effectively invisible.

**15. Position #1 organic gets 19x more clicks than the #1 paid result.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025

**16. Featured snippets earn a 42.9% CTR.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025. Learn how to [get featured snippets](/blog/get-featured-snippets) for your target keywords.

**17. Rich results earn 58% CTR vs. 41% for non-rich results.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025. [Schema markup](/blog/schema-markup-for-blog-posts) directly increases click rates.

**18. 58.5% of US Google searches result in zero clicks.**
Source: [Backlinko](https://backlinko.com/google-search-stats), 2025. Google answers more queries directly on the SERP through AI Overviews, knowledge panels, and featured snippets.

**19. 50% of Google searchers click a result within 9 seconds.**
Source: [AIOSEO](https://aioseo.com/seo-statistics/), 2025

## FAQ

**What is the most important SEO statistic in 2026?**

94% of web pages receive zero traffic from Google. That single stat explains why SEO strategy matters more than SEO volume. Publishing without optimization, [keyword research](/blog/keyword-research-for-blog-posts), and intent matching results in pages that never generate a single visit.

**How often are these SEO statistics updated?**

We update this page quarterly. The last update was March 2026. Every stat includes the source name, URL, and data year so you can verify the numbers independently.

**What is the ROI of SEO?**

SEO delivers a median ROI of 748%, or $22 for every $1 invested. Industry-specific ROI varies from 275% for average small businesses to 1,389% for real estate. The breakeven period is typically 7 to 10 months depending on competition and investment level.

**Is SEO still worth it in 2026?**

Yes. Despite the rise of AI search, organic search still drives 53.3% of all website traffic. 92% of marketers plan to maintain or increase SEO investment. The channel is changing, but the ROI data shows it remains the most cost-effective acquisition channel available.

**How has AI changed SEO statistics?**

AI search traffic grew 527% year-over-year. Google AI Overviews now reach 2 billion monthly users. Organic [CTR drops 61%](/glossary/click-through-rate) when AI Overviews appear. But AI referral visitors stay 38% longer and convert at higher rates. The traffic source is shifting, not disappearing.
