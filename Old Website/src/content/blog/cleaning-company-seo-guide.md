## Google Business Profile: Your Most Important Asset

Your [Google Business Profile](/blog/optimize-google-business-profile) (GBP) is the single most important ranking factor for local cleaning searches. GBP signals account for 32% of [local ranking factors](/glossary/local-ranking-factors). Your profile appears in the local pack, Google Maps, and AI Overviews before your website ever does.

### Set Up Your Profile Correctly

Choose "House Cleaning Service" or "Commercial Cleaning Service" as your primary category. Add secondary categories that match your services:

- Carpet Cleaning Service
- Window Cleaning Service
- Janitorial Service
- Maid Service
- Office Cleaning Service

### Complete Every Field

Incomplete profiles lose to complete ones. Fill in every section:

- **Business name:** Your exact legal name. Do not add keywords. "Sparkle Clean" is correct. "Sparkle Clean Best House Cleaning Austin TX" gets suspended.
- **Service areas:** List every city and zip code you serve. Google uses this data for "near me" searches.
- **Phone number:** Use a local number. It builds more trust than a toll-free number.
- **Business hours:** Keep these accurate. Update for holidays.
- **Business description:** 750 characters. Mention your city, services, and experience naturally.
- **Services:** Add each cleaning service individually with a brief description and price range.
- **Photos:** Upload 15+ photos. Before-and-after shots, team photos, branded vehicles, and equipment. Businesses with photos get 42% more requests for directions.

### Post to GBP Regularly

Post 2 to 3 times per week. Share cleaning tips, seasonal offers, before-and-after photos, and customer testimonials. Regular posting signals to Google that your business is active. For a complete strategy, see our [GBP posting frequency guide](/blog/gbp-posting-frequency).

### Respond to Every Review

Response rate matters for rankings. Reply to every review within 24 hours. Thank positive reviewers. Address negative reviews professionally. Use our [review response generator](/tools/review-response-generator) to save time while maintaining a personal tone.

## Service Pages That Convert

Your website needs a dedicated page for every cleaning service you offer. A single "Services" page with bullet points will not rank for anything specific. Google rewards pages that go deep on a single service.

![Essential service pages every cleaning company website needs](/images/blog/cleaning-company-service-pages.webp)

### Required Service Pages

Create a separate, optimized page for each:

- House cleaning / residential cleaning
- Deep cleaning
- Move-in and move-out cleaning
- Recurring cleaning (weekly, biweekly, monthly)
- Office cleaning
- Commercial cleaning / janitorial
- Post-construction cleaning
- Carpet cleaning
- Window cleaning
- Specialty cleaning (hoarding, Airbnb turnover, estate)

### What Each Service Page Needs

Every service page should include these elements:

- **Title tag:** "[Service] in [City] | [Company Name]" under 60 characters
- **H1 heading:** "[Service] in [Primary City]"
- **300+ words** describing the service, your process, what is included, and pricing guidance
- **Before-and-after photos** specific to that service type
- **Pricing information** (even ranges like "$150-$300 for a standard deep clean" build trust)
- **Customer testimonials** specific to that service
- **Clear CTA:** "Get a Free Quote" with phone number and booking form
- **[Schema markup](/blog/schema-markup-seo-guide)** using LocalBusiness and Service types
- **FAQ section** answering the 3-4 most common questions about that service

### City Landing Pages

If you serve multiple cities, create a dedicated page for each. A page targeting "house cleaning in Frisco TX" ranks better than a generic "house cleaning" page for Frisco searches.

Each city page must include:

- The city name in the title, H1, and body content
- Neighborhoods or zip codes within that city
- References to local details (landmarks, housing types, weather factors)
- Photos from jobs completed in that area
- Unique content (not copy-pasted with only the city name changed)

Google penalizes thin, duplicate city pages. Each page needs genuinely different content. This is where consistent [content publishing](/blog/seo-content-writing) at scale makes a real difference.

## Reviews: The Ranking Factor You Control

[Google reviews](/glossary/google-reviews) are a top-3 local ranking factor. They directly influence your local pack position and whether a homeowner chooses you over a competitor.

### Why Reviews Matter for Cleaning Companies

Cleaning is a trust-intensive service. You are entering someone's home. Customers want proof that you are reliable, thorough, and trustworthy before they hand over their keys.

- 93% of consumers read reviews before hiring a cleaning service
- Businesses with 50+ reviews generate 3x more leads
- A 4.5-star average is the sweet spot. Perfect 5.0 scores look suspicious.
- Review velocity matters. 2 to 4 new reviews per month signals ongoing activity.

### Build a Review Generation System

Do not wait for reviews to happen. Automate the process:

1. **Ask immediately after the clean.** Send a text message with a direct Google review link within 2 hours of job completion. Satisfaction is highest right after a spotless home.
2. **Use a QR code on your leave-behind card.** Many cleaning companies leave a "thank you" card after each visit. Add a QR code linking to your Google review page. Use our [review QR code generator](/tools/review-qr-code-generator).
3. **Follow up once.** A reminder 3 days after the initial request doubles the completion rate.
4. **Respond to every review.** Replies show potential customers that you are engaged and professional.
5. **Feature reviews on your website.** Embed your best reviews on service pages and your homepage.

## Technical SEO for Cleaning Websites

Technical problems block everything else from working. A slow site, broken mobile layout, or missing schema means your content and reviews cannot do their job.

### Site Speed

Cleaning company websites are often built on slow WordPress themes with large, uncompressed images. Compress every image. Use WebP format. Target under 3 seconds for full page load on mobile. Google's Core Web Vitals directly affect your rankings.

### Mobile Optimization

Over 60% of cleaning searches happen on mobile devices. Someone searching "house cleaning near me" while scrolling their phone needs to see your number, reviews, and booking button within 2 seconds of landing on your site. Test every page on a phone.

Key mobile checks:

- Phone number is tap-to-call
- Booking form works on mobile screens
- Text is readable without zooming
- Buttons are large enough to tap (44px minimum)
- No horizontal scrolling

### Schema Markup

Add [schema markup](/blog/schema-markup-seo-guide) so Google understands your business:

- **LocalBusiness** schema with name, address, phone, hours, and service areas
- **Service** schema for each cleaning service page
- **Review** schema to show star ratings in search results
- **FAQ** schema on pages with frequently asked questions

Schema earns rich snippets in search results. A listing with star ratings and pricing gets more clicks than plain text.

## Link Building for Cleaning Companies

Backlinks remain a top ranking factor. For cleaning companies, the best links come from local and industry-specific sources.

### Local Link Opportunities

- **Chamber of Commerce** membership (most include a website link)
- **Local sponsorships**. School events, charity drives, community clean-up days
- **Real estate agent partnerships**. Agents need move-in/out cleaning referrals. Offer a referral program and ask for a link on their "preferred vendors" page.
- **Property management companies**. Similar referral opportunity with a link exchange
- **Local news and blogs**. Offer cleaning tips for seasonal articles. Local lifestyle blogs love "spring cleaning" content.
- **Airbnb host communities**. Many Airbnb forums and local host groups link to recommended cleaning services

### Industry-Specific Links

- Cleaning trade associations (ISSA, ARCSI)
- Franchise directories (if applicable)
- Green cleaning certification directories
- Home service marketplaces that pass link equity

### Links to Avoid

- Paid link packages ("500 links for $99")
- Unrelated directories
- Private blog networks
- Excessive reciprocal exchanges

A few high-quality local links outperform hundreds of spammy ones. For more detail, read our guide on [building backlinks](/blog/build-backlinks-for-blog).
