## FAQ

**How much search traffic comes from AI?**

AI referral traffic currently accounts for about 1.08% of all website traffic, per [Conductor's 2026 benchmarks](https://www.conductor.com/academy/aeo-geo-benchmarks-report/). The volume grew 527% year over year. ChatGPT drives 87% of that traffic. Despite the small share, AI visitors convert at 14.2% compared to 2.8% for traditional organic visitors.

**What percentage of Google searches trigger AI Overviews?**

Approximately 50% of U.S. searches now trigger an AI Overview. Globally, the rate is closer to 25%. Informational queries trigger AI Overviews 99.2% of the time. Commercial and transactional queries trigger them far less frequently.

**Do AI Overviews reduce organic clicks?**

Yes. Organic CTR drops 61% on queries where AI Overviews appear. Position 1 CTR fell from 7.3% to 2.6% in one year. However, being cited within an AI Overview produces 35% more organic clicks than not being cited at all.

**How do I get cited by AI search engines?**

Focus on long-form content (2,900+ words), include statistics and citations, update content every 60 days, and build [domain authority](/blog/domain-authority-guide). Listicle format earns a 25% citation rate, the highest of any content type. [Schema markup](/blog/schema-markup-seo-guide) and structured answers also increase citation likelihood.

**Is AI search replacing Google?**

Not yet. Google still handles 77.9% of all digital queries. But ChatGPT holds 17.1% and growing. Among Gen Z, 66% use ChatGPT for research compared to 69% using Google. The gap is narrowing, especially for informational and creative queries.

**How fast is AI search traffic growing?**

AI referral traffic grows 165x faster than traditional organic search. Year-over-year growth exceeded 527%. During the 2025 holiday season, AI referral traffic surged 693%. The trajectory points to AI referrals representing 20 to 28% of all web referral traffic by end of 2026.
