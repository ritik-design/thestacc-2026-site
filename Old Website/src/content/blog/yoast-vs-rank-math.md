## What Is Yoast SEO?

Yoast SEO launched in 2010 and has been the default WordPress SEO plugin for over 15 years. It holds 10+ million active installations on WordPress.org, with a 4.8/5 rating from 27,792 reviews.

Yoast introduced the green/orange/red traffic light system that taught millions of WordPress users the basics of [on-page SEO](/blog/on-page-seo-guide). The Guardian, Microsoft, and Spotify use Yoast on their WordPress sites.

In 2021, Newfold Digital (parent company of Bluehost and HostGator) acquired Yoast. Since then, the plugin has added AI features, an AI+ subscription tier, and brand monitoring for AI search.

**Yoast is best for:** Content teams that need readability coaching, enterprise sites that value institutional trust, and beginners who want a simple traffic-light scoring system.

## What Is Rank Math?

Rank Math launched in November 2018 and now has 3+ million active installations. It grew roughly 300% since 2022 by offering most of Yoast Premium's features for free.

The plugin positions itself as a "Swiss army knife" for WordPress SEO. The free version includes redirect management, schema markup for 16+ content types, 404 monitoring, Google Search Console integration, and unlimited focus keywords per post.

Rank Math Pro ($83.88/year) adds a keyword rank tracker, advanced schema builder, and WooCommerce SEO tools for unlimited personal sites.

**Rank Math is best for:** Site owners who want maximum features without paying, agencies managing multiple sites, and SEOs who want built-in analytics and rank tracking.

## Schema Markup

[Schema markup](/blog/schema-markup-seo-guide) affects how your site appears in search results. Both plugins generate structured data, but their approaches differ.

### Rank Math Schema

Rank Math offers 16+ schema types in the free version. Pro adds 20+ types with an advanced schema builder. You can create custom schema, use schema templates for recurring patterns, and even import schema from other websites.

Free schema types include: Article, Product, Review, Recipe, FAQ, HowTo, Course, Event, Local Business, Video, Book, Person, Job Posting, Movie, Music, and Software Application.

The advanced schema builder (Pro) lets you create any custom JSON-LD markup without writing code.

### Yoast Schema

Yoast takes a different approach. Instead of offering many individual schema types, Yoast builds a Schema Graph. This connects Person, Organization, WebPage, and Article entities into an interconnected model.

The Schema Graph approach is technically rigorous and aligns well with Google's [E-E-A-T](/blog/eeat-google-quality-guide) signals. But the free version is limited to basic Article, FAQ (via Gutenberg blocks), and HowTo schema. Product schema requires the $178.80/year WooCommerce bundle.

### Schema Verdict

Rank Math wins on quantity and accessibility. Yoast wins on architectural rigor. For most WordPress sites, Rank Math's 16 free schema types cover every common use case. Enterprise sites that need entity-level schema sophistication may prefer Yoast's Graph model.

## AI Features

Both plugins have added AI capabilities. Their approaches are different.

### Rank Math Content AI

Rank Math offers a separate Content AI subscription at $47.88/year for 60,000 credits. It includes 40+ AI writing tools, 125+ prompt templates, and a RankBot assistant for research and content questions.

The catch: Content AI credits are not included with plugin plans. You get a 15-day trial with Pro/Business/Agency plans. After that, AI features stop unless you buy a separate Content AI subscription. Credits cost 1 per word generated and 500 per keyword research query. Credits do not roll over.

### Yoast AI Features

Yoast Premium includes AI Generate (creates 5 title and meta description options per click) and AI Optimize (automatically improves keyphrase placement in your content). No separate credit system. No per-word charges.

The AI+ Package ($358.80/year) adds AI Brand Insights. This unique feature tracks how your brand appears in answers from ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. No other WordPress SEO plugin offers this.

Yoast also provides a Google Docs add-on that lets you optimize content directly in Google Docs before publishing to WordPress.

### AI Verdict

Rank Math offers broader AI content creation tools. Yoast offers tighter integration with no credit headaches. If you want AI writing assistance inside WordPress, Rank Math Content AI gives you more options. If you want AI metadata optimization without managing credits, Yoast is simpler.

The AI Brand Insights feature in Yoast AI+ is unique and worth considering if [AI search visibility](/blog/track-ai-search-visibility) matters to your business.

## Readability Analysis

This is Yoast's strongest advantage. And most comparison articles understate how large the gap is.

### Yoast Readability

Yoast checks your content for:

- Flesch-Kincaid Reading Ease score
- Passive voice percentage
- Sentence length distribution
- Paragraph length
- Transition word usage
- Subheading distribution
- Consecutive sentence variety
- Inclusive language (age, gender, disability, socioeconomic status)

These checks work in 25+ languages. For content teams with non-SEO writers, this is the single most useful feature either plugin offers. It teaches writers to produce [SEO-friendly content](/blog/seo-content-writing) without needing SEO training.

### Rank Math Readability

Rank Math folds a basic readability score into its overall SEO score. It does not offer Flesch scoring, passive voice detection, transition word analysis, or inclusive language checks.

### Readability Verdict

Yoast wins by a wide margin. If your team relies on content readability coaching, Yoast is the better choice. If you handle readability yourself or use external editing tools, this feature gap does not matter.

## Migration: Switching Between Plugins

Both plugins offer one-click migration tools. Rank Math imports Yoast settings, focus keywords, meta titles, meta descriptions, and redirects automatically.

Key considerations before switching:

- **Backup first.** Always create a full site backup before deactivating any SEO plugin
- **Meta data transfers cleanly.** Titles, descriptions, and focus keywords import without issues
- **Schema may need review.** Custom schema configurations do not always translate perfectly
- **Redirects import.** Rank Math can import Yoast redirect rules during setup
- **Rankings are not affected.** Both plugins generate the same HTML output for search engines. Google does not care which plugin produces the meta tags. Your [keyword rankings](/blog/keyword-optimization-guide) will not change from switching alone

The migration process takes under 5 minutes for most sites. Rank Math's setup wizard handles the import during initial configuration.

## FAQ

**Is Rank Math better than Yoast SEO?**

For most WordPress users, yes. Rank Math's free version includes features that Yoast locks behind its $99/year premium plan. The exception is content teams that rely on Yoast's readability analysis. That feature has no equivalent in Rank Math.

**Will I lose rankings if I switch from Yoast to Rank Math?**

No. Both plugins generate identical HTML output for search engines. Google does not know or care which plugin produces your meta tags. Rankings depend on content quality and backlinks, not which SEO plugin you use.

**Can I use Yoast and Rank Math at the same time?**

No. Running two SEO plugins simultaneously causes duplicate meta tags, conflicting schema markup, and XML sitemap conflicts. Use one or the other. Never both.

**Is the Rank Math free version good enough?**

For single-site owners, yes. The free version includes unlimited focus keywords, redirect management, 16+ schema types, image SEO, and Google Search Console integration. The Pro upgrade adds rank tracking and advanced schema, which are nice but not essential for most sites.

**Is Yoast Premium worth $99/year?**

Only if you specifically need Yoast's readability analysis combined with redirect management and multiple focus keywords. Rank Math offers comparable redirect management and unlimited focus keywords for free. The readability coaching is the only feature with no free alternative.

**Which plugin is better for WooCommerce?**

Rank Math. Basic product schema and WooCommerce support are free. Yoast requires a $178.80/year WooCommerce bundle. Rank Math Pro ($83.88/year) adds GTIN/MPN schema, WooCommerce sitemaps, and product page optimization for less than half the cost.
