> **Google AI Overviews now appear on 48% of all search queries.** That is a 58% increase year over year. Organic CTR drops 34-61% when an AI Overview appears. These numbers are reshaping every SEO strategy.

Google AI Overviews have gone from an experiment to a dominant search feature in under 2 years. The data shows a clear pattern: more AI Overviews, fewer clicks to websites, and a fundamental shift in how organic traffic works.

## General AI Overview Adoption Statistics

**1. AI Overviews appear on 48% of all Google search queries as of March 2026.**
(Source: Advanced Web Ranking / Digital Applied, 2026)
Up from 34.5% in December 2025 and 31% in February 2025. The growth rate is accelerating.

**2. AI Overviews grew 58% year over year across all tracked queries.**
(Source: Digital Applied, 2026)
The expansion hit every major industry category from healthcare to ecommerce.

**3. AI Overviews appear on 60.32% of U.S. queries according to some tracking methods.**
(Source: Advanced Web Ranking, 2026)
Measurement methodology affects the number. Different tools report 18-60% depending on query sample and geographic scope.

**4. AI Overviews reach 2 billion monthly users globally.**
(Source: DemandSage, 2026)
Google rolled AI Overviews out to 200+ countries and territories.

**5. 57% of long-tail queries trigger AI Overviews.**
(Source: Semrush, 2025)
Informational long-tail queries are the most affected. Short head terms trigger AI Overviews less frequently.

**6. 52% of queries still trigger no AI Overview at all.**
(Source: Digital Applied, 2026)
Classic ten-blue-link results remain the majority experience for roughly half of all searches.

**7. Informational queries trigger AI Overviews 39.4% of the time.**
(Source: Semrush, 2026)
Commercial and transactional queries trigger them less often.

## CTR and Click-Through Rate Impact

**8. Organic CTR drops 61% when an AI Overview is present.**
(Source: Seer Interactive, September 2025)
CTR fell from 1.76% to 0.61% for queries with AI Overviews. The largest single-study CTR decline measured.

**9. Paid CTR drops 68% when an AI Overview appears.**
(Source: Seer Interactive, September 2025)
Paid CTR crashed from 19.7% to 6.34%. AI Overviews hurt paid search even more than organic.

**10. Position 1 rankings lose 34.5% of their CTR when AI Overviews appear.**
(Source: [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-overviews-reduce-clicks-update/), 2025)
Measured across 300,000 keywords. The top organic result absorbs the biggest CTR loss.

**11. AI Overviews reduce average CTR by 58% for top-ranking pages.**
(Source: Ahrefs, December 2025)
Analyzed across 863,000 keywords and 4 million AI Overview URLs.

**12. Non-branded queries lose 19.98% CTR when AI Overviews appear.**
(Source: Amsive Digital, 2025)
Branded queries are less affected because users already intend to visit a specific site.

**13. CTR drops 15.49% on average across 700,000 keywords studied.**
(Source: Amsive Digital, 2025)
The lower-bound estimate. Some query categories see minimal impact.

**14. Only 1% of users click the links cited within AI Overviews.**
(Source: Multiple studies, 2025)
Despite being prominently featured, citation links inside AI Overviews receive almost no clicks.

**15. CTR drops 34-46% depending on query type and study methodology.**
(Source: Multiple independent studies, 2024-2025)
The range reflects different measurement approaches. All studies agree the direction is negative.

## FAQ

**What is the most important Google AI Overview statistic in 2026?**

The 61% organic CTR decline measured by Seer Interactive is the most significant finding. It confirms that AI Overviews fundamentally reduce clicks to websites for queries where they appear. This single stat shapes every content strategy decision.

**How often do Google AI Overviews appear in search results?**

AI Overviews trigger on approximately 48% of all Google search queries as of March 2026. The rate varies by query type: 88% for healthcare, 83% for education, and 37% for entertainment. Informational queries trigger AI Overviews 39.4% of the time.

**Do AI Overviews help or hurt website traffic?**

Both. Overall, AI Overviews reduce organic CTR by 34-61%. But brands cited within AI Overviews earn 35% more organic clicks and 91% more paid clicks compared to uncited brands.
