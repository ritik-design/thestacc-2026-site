## GBP Usage and Adoption Statistics

**1. 18 million American businesses use Google Business Profile.**
(Source: Google, 2025)
That covers roughly half of all U.S. businesses with employees.

**2. Only 64% of businesses have verified their Google Business Profile.**
(Source: [Birdeye](https://birdeye.com/blog/state-of-google-business-profiles/), 2025)

**3. Companies edit their Google Business Profiles 15 million times per month.**
(Source: Google, 2025)

**4. 83% of property management companies have verified GBPs. That is the highest rate by industry.**
(Source: Birdeye, 2025)
Property management leads all sectors in GBP adoption.

**5. Only 45% of automotive and home services businesses have verified GBPs. That is the lowest rate.**
(Source: Birdeye, 2025)
The industries that benefit most from local search often have the lowest adoption rates.

**6. 60% of businesses have never posted on their Google Business Profile.**
(Source: Birdeye, 2025)
Most businesses miss the engagement boost that [regular GBP posting](/blog/optimize-google-business-profile) provides.

**7. 33% of verified profiles use Google Messages.**
(Source: Birdeye, 2025)
Messaging adoption varies by industry. Legal (67%) and consumer goods (63%) lead.

**8. There are 4,039 Google Business Profile categories as of March 2026.**
(Source: [Dalton Luka](https://daltonluka.com/blog/google-my-business-categories), 2026)
Google updates categories frequently. Choosing the right primary category directly impacts ranking.

## FAQ

**How many businesses use Google Business Profile?**

18 million American businesses use Google Business Profile. That represents roughly half of all U.S. businesses with employees. Only 64% have verified their profiles, which means millions of businesses miss ranking features and full visibility.

**How many clicks does a Google Business Profile get?**

The average verified GBP receives approximately 200 clicks per month, including 105 website visits, 50 phone calls, and 66 direction requests. Profiles with complete information and 100+ photos see dramatically higher engagement.

**Do Google reviews affect local rankings?**

Yes. Top-ranking local businesses average 47 reviews, compared to 39 for the average business. [Review volume](/blog/google-reviews-statistics), recency (73% of consumers only trust reviews from the past month), and response rate all factor into local search rankings.

**How important are GBP photos for local SEO?**

Extremely important. Profiles with photos receive 42% more direction requests and 35% more website clicks. Businesses with 100+ photos see 520% more calls. Top-ranking profiles average 250 images. See our [GBP photo guide](/blog/gbp-photos-guide) for optimization tips.

**What percentage of local ranking depends on GBP?**

GBP signals account for 32% of local pack ranking factors, making it the single largest ranking factor. Profile completeness, reviews, photos, categories, and posting frequency all contribute to this 32% weight.

**How often should I update my Google Business Profile?**

At minimum, weekly. Post new content, respond to reviews, add photos, and update business information. 60% of businesses never post on their GBP. The businesses that post regularly outperform those that treat their profile as a set-and-forget listing.
