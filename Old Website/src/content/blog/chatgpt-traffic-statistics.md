## General ChatGPT Traffic Statistics

**1. ChatGPT recorded 5.723 billion total visits in January 2026.**
Source: [DemandSage](https://www.demandsage.com/chatgpt-statistics/), 2026
That makes it one of the 10 most-visited websites on the planet, ahead of X and Wikipedia.

**2. February 2026 traffic dropped to 5.35 billion visits, a 6.5% month-over-month decline.**
Source: [DemandSage](https://www.demandsage.com/chatgpt-statistics/), 2026
Seasonal dips are normal. January typically sees elevated usage after holiday downtime.

**3. ChatGPT peaked at 6.165 billion visits in October 2025.**
Source: [Incremys](https://www.incremys.com/en/resources/blog/chatgpt-statistics), 2025
That represented a 4.43% increase over September 2025.

**4. September 2025 traffic reached 5.8 billion visits, up 7.6% month over month.**
Source: [Superlines](https://www.superlines.io/articles/chatgpt-statistics/), 2025
Growth accelerated after the launch of new model capabilities.

**5. ChatGPT averages 193.33 million visits per day as of February 2026.**
Source: [Index.dev](https://www.index.dev/blog/chatgpt-statistics), 2026
That translates to roughly 8 million visits per hour.

**6. ChatGPT ranks #6 globally and #1 in the AI Chatbots category on Similarweb.**
Source: [Similarweb](https://www.similarweb.com/website/chatgpt.com/), 2026
It sits behind Google, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Reddit.

**7. ChatGPT rivals Instagram and surpasses X and Wikipedia in total web traffic.**
Source: [Incremys](https://www.incremys.com/en/resources/blog/chatgpt-statistics), 2025
Two years ago, ChatGPT was not in the top 50. The growth rate is unprecedented.

## ChatGPT vs Google Search Statistics

**29. ChatGPT processes approximately 12% of Google Search volume.**
Source: [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/chatgpt-has-12-percent-of-googles-search-volume/), 2025
That is a massive number for a product that did not exist 4 years ago.

**30. Google sends 190 times more traffic to websites than ChatGPT.**
Source: [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/chatgpt-has-12-percent-of-googles-search-volume/), 2025
ChatGPT processes many queries but keeps users inside its interface. Google still drives the vast majority of referral traffic.

**31. Google makes up approximately 40% of all traffic to websites. ChatGPT accounts for just 0.21%.**
Source: [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/chatgpt-has-12-percent-of-googles-search-volume/), 2025
This is the critical distinction. Volume does not equal referral traffic. For a deeper look at this shift, read our [AI search referral traffic data](/blog/ai-search-referral-traffic-stats).

**32. Google receives 83.9 billion global visits per month. That is 14 times ChatGPT traffic.**
Source: [Momentic](https://momenticmarketing.com/blog/google-reach-chatgpt-click-throughs), 2025
Google remains dominant in raw reach. But the gap is narrowing.

**33. The average ChatGPT user clicks 1.4 external links per visit. Google users click 0.6 per visit.**
Source: [Momentic](https://momenticmarketing.com/blog/google-reach-chatgpt-click-throughs), 2025
When ChatGPT does send traffic out, the click-through rate per user is higher than Google.

**34. 64% of all ChatGPT referrals land on just 120 domains.**
Source: [Momentic](https://momenticmarketing.com/blog/google-reach-chatgpt-click-throughs), 2025
ChatGPT referral traffic is extremely concentrated. Wikipedia alone receives [47.9% of ChatGPT citations](/blog/wikipedia-chatgpt-citations). Getting [cited by AI search engines](/blog/get-cited-ai-search) requires specific optimization.

| Metric | Google Search | ChatGPT |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly visits | 83.9 billion | ~5.7 billion |
| Share of web traffic sent | ~40% | ~0.21% |
| External links clicked per visit | 0.6 | 1.4 |
| Top referral domains | Millions | ~120 |

## FAQ

**How many people use ChatGPT in 2026?**

ChatGPT has 900 million weekly active users and approximately 210 million daily active users as of early 2026. Monthly visits to chatgpt.com exceed 5 billion.

**How much traffic does ChatGPT get per month?**

ChatGPT receives between 5.3 and 6.1 billion monthly visits depending on the month. January 2026 recorded 5.7 billion. October 2025 peaked at 6.1 billion.

**Is ChatGPT bigger than Google?**

No. Google receives 83.9 billion visits per month, roughly 14 times more than ChatGPT. However, ChatGPT processes approximately 12% of Google search volume, and that gap is closing. For more context, see our [Google search statistics](/blog/google-search-statistics) roundup.

**How much money does ChatGPT make?**

OpenAI reached $10 billion in annual recurring revenue as of mid-2025. ChatGPT generates approximately 66% of that, or about $8 billion annually. Plus subscriptions at $20/month are the largest revenue source.

**What percentage of Fortune 500 companies use ChatGPT?**

92% of Fortune 500 companies use ChatGPT. OpenAI has deployed 7 million enterprise seats across approximately 1.5 million business customers.

**Does ChatGPT send traffic to websites?**

Very little. ChatGPT accounts for only 0.21% of all website traffic, compared to Google at 40%. However, when ChatGPT does link out, users click at a rate of 1.4 links per visit versus 0.6 on Google.
