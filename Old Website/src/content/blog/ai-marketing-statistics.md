## AI Marketing Adoption Statistics

**1. 88% of marketers use AI tools daily in 2026.**
Source: [HubSpot State of Marketing Report](https://www.hubspot.com/state-of-marketing), 2026
Daily usage, not occasional experimentation. AI has moved from optional to default in most marketing workflows.

**2. 76% of marketing teams use AI in core operations.**
Source: [CoSchedule State of AI in Marketing](https://coschedule.com/ai-marketing-statistics), 2025
Three in 4 teams run AI across their primary functions. That number was under 40% just 3 years ago.

**3. 86% of SEO professionals have integrated AI into their workflows.**
Source: [Semrush](https://www.semrush.com/blog/ai-seo-statistics/), 2026
SEO teams adopted AI faster than any other marketing function. Learn more in our [SEO statistics roundup](/blog/seo-statistics).

**4. 85% of marketers use AI for content creation.**
Source: [CoSchedule](https://coschedule.com/ai-marketing-statistics), 2025
Content writing is the top use case. Read our guide on [using AI to write blog posts](/blog/use-ai-write-blog-posts) for practical workflows.

**5. Only 4% of companies are unwilling to adopt AI.**
Source: [CoSchedule](https://coschedule.com/ai-marketing-statistics), 2025
39% are very open to adoption. Another 31% are open. Resistance is now a fringe position.

**6. 90% of marketing teams plan to increase AI integration within 2 to 3 years.**
Source: [CoSchedule](https://coschedule.com/ai-marketing-statistics), 2025
Nearly half (47%) expect significant increases. The shift is accelerating, not plateauing.

**7. AI adoption in marketing grew by 36 percentage points between 2022 and 2025.**
Source: [Amra and Elma](https://www.amraandelma.com/ai-marketing-tool-adoption-statistics/), 2025
That is the fastest adoption curve of any marketing technology in the past decade.

**8. 93% of marketers report new AI features added to their existing tools in 2024.**
Source: [CoSchedule](https://coschedule.com/ai-marketing-statistics), 2025
42% say most or all of their tools now include AI features. Adoption is happening passively through tool updates.

**45. 68% of marketers use AI but only 17% receive training.**
