## FAQ

**What is the ROI of content marketing?**

Content marketing returns an average of $3 for every $1 spent, compared to $1.80 for paid advertising. Some industries see higher returns. SaaS content marketing averages 19:1 ROI. Email marketing delivers $36 to $42 per $1 spent. The ROI depends on consistency, quality, and measurement.

**How many businesses use content marketing?**

91% of B2B marketers use content marketing. 80% of businesses use blogs as a marketing tool. The adoption rate is near-universal among growth-focused companies.

**What percentage of marketers use AI for content?**

89% of marketers now use AI for content creation. 97% of content marketers plan to use AI in their workflows. The most common uses are ideation (74%), outlining (61%), and drafting (44%).

**Is blogging still effective in 2026?**

Yes. Businesses that blog generate 55% more traffic and 67% more leads. 7.5 million blog posts are published daily, which means competition is high, but businesses that prioritize [SEO content writing](/blog/seo-content-writing) and publish consistently are 13x more likely to see positive ROI.

**How often should a business publish content?**

The data supports consistent publishing over sporadic bursts. Businesses publishing 6 to 8 SEO-optimized posts per month build effective topic clusters within 6 to 12 months. Quality and keyword targeting matter more than raw volume.
