## Global Social Media Usage Statistics

![Global social media usage statistics for 2026](/images/blog/social-media-statistics-global-usage.webp)

**1. 5.66 billion people use social media worldwide in 2026.**
Source: [DataReportal, January 2026](https://datareportal.com/global-digital-overview)
That is 68.7% of the world population. The number grew by 256 million users in the past year.

**2. The average person spends 2 hours and 21 minutes per day on social media.**
Source: [DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/global-digital-overview)
Daily time has remained stable since 2023, suggesting social media usage has reached a plateau in mature markets.

**3. The average user accesses 6.75 different social media platforms per month.**
Source: [DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/global-digital-overview)
Multi-platform usage means brands cannot rely on a single channel. Build a [social media calendar](/blog/create-social-media-calendar) that covers at least 3 platforms.

**4. 94.5% of internet users are active on social media.**
Source: [DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/global-digital-overview)
Social media is effectively synonymous with internet use for all practical marketing purposes.

**5. Social media now surpasses Google as the primary product discovery channel for consumers under 35.**
Source: [HubSpot State of Marketing, 2026](https://www.hubspot.com/state-of-marketing)
This shift accelerated in 2025 as TikTok Search and Instagram Shopping matured.

**6. 78% of consumers have purchased a product they discovered on social media.**
Source: [Sprout Social, 2026](https://sproutsocial.com/insights/social-media-statistics/)
Social is no longer just awareness. It drives revenue directly.

**7. 63% of social media users say they trust recommendations from creators more than brand ads.**
Source: [Hootsuite Social Trends 2026](https://www.hootsuite.com/research/social-trends)
Creator partnerships outperform paid ads for trust and engagement in most consumer categories.

**8. Mobile devices account for 82.9% of all social media usage.**
Source: [Statista, 2026](https://www.statista.com/topics/779/mobile-internet/)
Every piece of social content must be designed mobile-first. Desktop-first design is a legacy approach.

## Platform User Statistics

Facebook remains the largest social platform by total users. Growth is driven by Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

**10. Instagram has surpassed 3 billion monthly active users.**
Source: [Meta Q4 2025 Earnings](https://investor.fb.com/)
Instagram crossed the 3 billion mark in late 2025. Reels drive 50%+ of time spent on the platform.

**11. TikTok has approximately 1.9 billion monthly active users.**
Source: [DataReportal / Business of Apps, 2026](https://www.businessofapps.com/data/tik-tok-statistics/)
TikTok grew 12% year over year despite regulatory pressure in North America and Europe.

**13. LinkedIn has reached 1.3 billion total members.**
Source: [LinkedIn About Page, 2026](https://about.linkedin.com/)

**14. X (formerly Twitter) has approximately 557 million monthly active users.**

**16. Snapchat reaches 474 million daily active users.**
Source: [Snap Q4 2025 Earnings](https://investor.snap.com/)

**17. Threads has surpassed 400 million signups.**

**18. Reddit reaches 765 million monthly active users.**
Source: [Reddit Q4 2025 Earnings](https://investor.redditinc.com/)
Reddit is increasingly important for SEO. Google now surfaces Reddit discussions in search results at high frequency.

## Social Media Demographics

![Social media demographics and age breakdown statistics 2026](/images/blog/social-media-statistics-demographics.webp)

**19. Gen Z spends an average of 5 hours per day on social media.**
Source: [GWI, 2026](https://www.gwi.com/)
This is more than double the overall average. Gen Z uses social media as their primary entertainment, news, and shopping channel.

**20. 33% of US teenagers say they use social media "almost constantly."**
Source: [Pew Research Center, 2025](https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/2025/01/social-media-and-teens/)
Up from 24% in 2022. The trend toward always-on social usage shows no signs of reversing.

**21. Adults ages 50-64 are the fastest-growing social media demographic.**
Source: [Pew Research Center, 2025](https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/)
Facebook and YouTube drive this growth. Older adults now represent a significant and underserved advertising audience.

**22. 54.4% of social media users worldwide are male. 45.6% are female.**
Source: [DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/global-digital-overview)
The gender gap has narrowed significantly since 2020. Platform-level differences remain larger than the aggregate.

**23. India has the largest social media user base at 534 million users.**
Source: [Statista, 2026](https://www.statista.com/statistics/272014/global-social-networks-ranked-by-number-of-users/)
India surpassed China (where many global platforms are restricted) as the top market by total users.

**24. TikTok skews young: 67% of users are under 35.**
Source: [DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/global-digital-overview)
The 18-24 age group represents 36% of TikTok users. Brands targeting Gen Z cannot ignore TikTok.

**25. LinkedIn users are 60% between the ages of 25 and 34.**
Source: [LinkedIn, 2026](https://about.linkedin.com/)
The platform skews toward working professionals in their early career and mid-career stages.

**26. 49% of Facebook users are over age 35.**
Source: [Statista, 2026](https://www.statista.com/statistics/187549/facebook-distribution-of-users-age-group-usa/)
Facebook is aging. Brands targeting consumers under 25 see better results on TikTok and Instagram.

## Engagement Rates by Platform

![Social media engagement rates by platform for 2026](/images/blog/social-media-statistics-engagement.webp)

**27. TikTok average engagement rate: 3.70% (up 49% year over year).**
Source: [Social Insider, 2026](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks/)
TikTok dominates engagement across all platforms. Short-form video earns 2.5x more engagement than static posts.

**28. LinkedIn average engagement rate: 6.5% for document (carousel) posts.**
Source: [Social Insider, 2026](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks/)
LinkedIn carousels outperform every other post type on the platform by a wide margin.

**29. Instagram average engagement rate: 0.48%.**
Source: [Social Insider, 2026](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks/)
Reels drive significantly higher engagement (2-3x) compared to static image posts and Stories.

**30. Facebook average engagement rate: 0.15%.**
Source: [Social Insider, 2026](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks/)
Organic reach on Facebook continues to decline. Paid amplification is required for meaningful brand page visibility.

**31. X (Twitter) average engagement rate: 0.12%.**
Source: [Social Insider, 2026](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks/)
X has the lowest average engagement of any major platform. Threads and reply chains outperform standalone posts.

**32. Video posts generate 2.5x more engagement than image posts across all platforms.**
Source: [Buffer State of Social Media Engagement, 2026](https://buffer.com/resources/state-of-social-media-engagement-2026/)
Every platform algorithm now prioritizes video content. Teams not producing video are competing with a handicap.

**33. Posts published between 9 AM and 12 PM local time earn the highest engagement.**
Source: [Hootsuite, 2026](https://www.hootsuite.com/research/social-trends)
Morning engagement peaks hold across LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram. TikTok peaks shift later (6-9 PM).

**34. The average organic reach for a Facebook page post is 5.2% of total followers.**
Source: [Hootsuite, 2026](https://www.hootsuite.com/research/social-trends)
Down from 5.5% in 2024. Brands with [social media automation tools](/best/social-media-automation-tools) publish more consistently, which compounds reach over time.

## Social Media Advertising Statistics

![Social media advertising spend and benchmark statistics 2026](/images/blog/social-media-statistics-ad-spend.webp)

**43. Global social media advertising spend reached $317 billion in 2026.**
Source: [Statista, 2026](https://www.statista.com/outlook/dmo/digital-advertising/social-media-advertising/worldwide)
Social ad spend grew 14% year over year. It now represents 33% of all digital advertising.

**44. Facebook/Meta average CPM: $7.19.**
Source: [Revealbot, 2026](https://revealbot.com/facebook-ad-benchmarks)
CPM varies by industry. Finance and insurance see $15+ CPMs. Retail averages $4-6.

**45. Instagram average CPM: $8.30.**
Source: [Revealbot, 2026](https://revealbot.com/facebook-ad-benchmarks)
Reels ads deliver 20-30% lower CPMs than feed ads, making them the most cost-effective Instagram ad format.

**46. TikTok average CPM: $10.00.**
Source: [DataReportal / TikTok for Business, 2026](https://datareportal.com/global-digital-overview)
TikTok CPMs have risen 25% as advertiser demand outpaces inventory. Still delivers strong engagement per impression.

**47. LinkedIn average CPC: $5.26.**
Source: [LinkedIn Marketing Solutions, 2026](https://business.linkedin.com/)
LinkedIn is the most expensive platform per click. The tradeoff: LinkedIn clicks convert to B2B leads at 3x the rate of other platforms.

**48. Average Facebook ad click-through rate: 0.90%.**
Source: [WordStream, 2026](https://www.wordstream.com/blog/ws/facebook-advertising-benchmarks)
CTR has declined year over year as feed saturation increases. Creative quality and targeting precision matter more than ever.

**49. Social media ad revenue on mobile devices accounts for 82.9% of total social ad spend.**
Source: [Statista, 2026](https://www.statista.com/topics/779/mobile-internet/)
Mobile-first ad creative is non-negotiable. Vertical video (9:16) outperforms horizontal by 40% on mobile.

**50. The average conversion rate for social media ads is 1.5-2.5%.**
Source: [WordStream, 2026](https://www.wordstream.com/blog/ws/facebook-advertising-benchmarks)
Ecommerce conversions average higher (2.5-4%). B2B lead generation averages lower (0.8-1.5%).
