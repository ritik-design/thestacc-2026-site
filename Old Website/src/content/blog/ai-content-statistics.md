## FAQ

**What percentage of online content is AI-generated?**

As of 2025, [74.2% of newly created web pages](https://ahrefs.com/blog/what-percentage-of-new-content-is-ai-generated/) contain some AI-generated content. Only 2.5% are entirely AI-generated with no human editing. 71.7% use a human-AI blend. Gartner predicted that 90% of online content could be AI-generated by 2026.

**Does Google penalize AI-generated content?**

No. Ahrefs found a near-zero correlation (0.011) between AI content and ranking penalties across 600,000 pages. Google penalizes low-quality content regardless of origin. The risk is "scaled content abuse" where low-quality AI pages are published at volume. Quality AI content with human oversight ranks normally.

**How much money does AI save on content creation?**

AI-produced content costs up to 4.7 times less than human-only content. Marketers save an average of 2.5 hours per day. Teams using AI daily report average ROI of 300% with a 37% reduction in customer acquisition costs. The savings are largest for high-volume blog production.

**Can consumers tell if content is AI-generated?**

82.1% of U.S. consumers say they notice AI content. But AI detection tools struggle. Only 5 out of 12+ popular tools scored above 70% accuracy. The FTC found one detection company's actual accuracy was 53% despite claiming 98%. The gap between consumer perception and detection tool accuracy is significant.

**What is the most important AI content statistic for 2026?**

The most actionable statistic: [74.2% of new web pages contain AI content](https://ahrefs.com/blog/what-percentage-of-new-content-is-ai-generated/), but only 2.5% are pure AI. The winning approach is human-AI collaboration, not full automation. Sites using AI with human editors saw 73% lower bounce rates and 36% higher conversions.
