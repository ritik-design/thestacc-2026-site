## FAQ

**What are the best marketing tool directories for SaaS products?**

G2, Capterra, and Software Advice drive the most buyer traffic for SaaS products. These three platforms combined receive 10M+ monthly visits from buyers actively comparing software. Start with these, then expand to niche directories specific to your product category.

**Are software directory listings free?**

Most directories offer free basic listings. G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, AlternativeTo, and Product Hunt all accept free submissions. Paid tiers on G2 and Capterra unlock enhanced visibility, featured placements, and lead generation features. Start free and upgrade once you see ROI.

**Do marketing tool directories help with SEO?**

Yes. Directory listings on high-DA platforms like G2 (DA 90+), Capterra (DA 85+), and Product Hunt (DA 90+) generate valuable backlinks. According to [Ahrefs' backlink study](https://ahrefs.com/blog/search-traffic-study/), diversifying your backlink profile across trusted directories strengthens your overall [domain authority](/glossary/domain-authority).

**How many software directories should I list on?**

Start with the top 10 general directories (G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, AlternativeTo, SaaSHub). Then add 5-10 niche directories specific to your product category. Quality of listing matters more than quantity. A well-optimized G2 profile with 50+ reviews outperforms 100 thin listings with no reviews.

**What is the difference between agency directories and freelancer directories?**

Agency directories (Clutch, DesignRush, UpCity) connect companies with service firms. [Freelancer directories](/blog/content-marketing-strategy) (Upwork, Toptal, Fiverr) connect companies with individual professionals. Agencies should list on agency directories. Solo consultants and freelancers should list on freelancer marketplaces.

**How long does it take to see traffic from directory listings?**

Referral traffic starts within days of listing on high-traffic platforms like G2 and Product Hunt. SEO benefits from directory backlinks appear in 60 to 90 days. Review-based platforms drive steady traffic for months because buyers search them continuously. The more reviews you collect, the more traffic you receive.
