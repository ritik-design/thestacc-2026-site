## ChatGPT Statistics (2026)

ChatGPT remains the dominant AI search platform. But its lead is narrowing at an accelerating pace.

**1. ChatGPT holds 64.5% of AI chatbot traffic share.**
That figure is down from 86.7% one year prior, according to [The Digital Bloom](https://thedigitalbloom.com/learn/gen-ai-website-traffic-share-february-2026/). That is a 22-point drop in 12 months.

**2. ChatGPT drives 87.4% of all AI referral traffic to websites.**
Despite its shrinking chatbot share, ChatGPT still dominates when it comes to actual clicks out to websites. Data from [Conductor's AEO/GEO Benchmarks Report](https://conductor.com/academy/aeo-geo-benchmarks-report/) shows no other platform comes close on referral volume.

**3. ChatGPT has 883M monthly users and 5.4B global monthly visits.**
[Exposure Ninja](https://exposureninja.com/blog/ai-search-statistics/) places its reach at a scale larger than most traditional media properties.

**4. Weekly active users reached 900M.**
[First Page Sage](https://firstpagesage.com/seo-blog/chatgpt-usage-statistics/) reported this figure for early 2026, placing ChatGPT among the most-used applications in internet history.

**5. ChatGPT's app market share fell from 69.1% to 45.3%.**
[Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/02/05/chatgpt-openai-market-share-app-slip-google-rivals-close-the-gap/) reported this mobile app share decline. Competitors are closing the gap on every front.

The referral traffic advantage ChatGPT holds is critical for SEO strategy. Even as its share of chatbot conversations falls, it still sends the most visitors to external sites. Learn how to [track AI search visibility](/blog/track-ai-search-visibility) before you optimize for it.

## Google Gemini Statistics (2026)

Gemini is the fastest-growing platform in AI search. Its growth is not incremental. It is structural, driven by Google's integration of Gemini across Search, Android, and Workspace.

**6. Gemini holds 21.5% AI chatbot market share, up from 5.7% one year ago.**
That represents [647% year-over-year growth](https://sedestral.com/en/blog/ai-search-market-share-2026). No other platform in the AI search category has grown at this rate.

**7. Gemini receives 2B monthly visits and has 650M monthly active users.**
[Sedestral's 2026 AI market share report](https://sedestral.com/en/blog/ai-search-market-share-2026) places Gemini as a clear second to ChatGPT in raw traffic volume.

**8. Gemini's mobile app share grew from 14.7% to 25.2%.**
[Vertu's analysis](https://vertu.com/lifestyle/ai-chatbot-market-share-2026-chatgpt-drops-to-68-as-google-gemini-surges-to-18-2/) tracked this shift over 12 months. As ChatGPT's app share fell, Gemini absorbed much of the difference.

## Perplexity AI Statistics (2026)

Perplexity is the most search-native AI platform. It is built specifically to answer queries with cited sources, which makes it both a competitor to Google and a potential referral driver for content-rich sites.

**9. Perplexity holds between 6.4% and 8% of the AI chatbot market.**
[Seoprofy's Perplexity statistics report](https://seoprofy.com/blog/perplexity-ai-statistics/) places it as the clear third-largest platform by market share.

**10. Perplexity has 45M monthly active users and 170M monthly visitors.**
For context: Perplexity generates roughly 8x its MAU count in monthly visits, suggesting a high frequency of use per active user. Source: [Seoprofy](https://seoprofy.com/blog/perplexity-ai-statistics/).

**11. Perplexity includes links in 77% or more of its responses.**
This is the highest link citation rate of any major AI search platform. [Spotlight's research](https://get-spotlight.com/articles/how-to-measure-and-improve-brand-share-of-voice-in-ai-chatbots-and-llms-a-step-by-step-guide/) shows Perplexity is architecturally built to surface and cite sources.

## AI Referral Traffic Statistics

The market share numbers describe who uses which chatbot. The referral data describes what AI search actually does to your website traffic. And it is more complicated than most analytics dashboards show.

![AI referral traffic statistics for marketers](/images/blog/ai-referral-traffic-stats.webp)

**15. AI referral traffic represents 1.08% of total web traffic.**
Small in percentage terms. But the quality of that traffic is unusual, per [Conductor's benchmarks report](https://conductor.com/academy/aeo-geo-benchmarks-report/).

**16. AI-referred visitors convert at 2x the rate of average web visitors.**
Conductor found this conversion premium across a large sample of websites. AI search users arrive with high intent and a specific goal. They are not browsing. They are deciding.

**17. AI visitors complete meaningful sessions in 1 out of every 3 visits.**
That session engagement rate is also documented in [Conductor's AEO/GEO Benchmarks Report](https://conductor.com/academy/aeo-geo-benchmarks-report/). These visitors read, explore, and act.

**18. 70.6% of AI referral traffic is misclassified as "direct" in GA4.**
This is the most consequential stat for marketers. [The Digital Bloom's February 2026 analysis](https://thedigitalbloom.com/learn/gen-ai-website-traffic-share-february-2026/) found that most AI-originated sessions appear as direct traffic in standard analytics setups. Your AI referral numbers are almost certainly undercounted.

**19. Google still accounts for approximately 80% of all digital queries.**
[First Page Sage's comparative report](https://firstpagesage.com/seo-blog/google-vs-chatgpt-market-share-report/) confirms that traditional search is not collapsing. AI search is additive to the existing query ecosystem, not yet a replacement.

The misclassification problem is why building share of model visibility as a tracked metric matters now. Read our post on [share of model visibility](/blog/share-of-model-visibility) for a practical measurement framework.

The data above points to 4 actionable conclusions for any business publishing content online.

**1. Optimize for ChatGPT and Perplexity first.**
ChatGPT sends 87.4% of AI referral clicks. Perplexity cites sources in 77%+ of responses. These two platforms produce the most measurable inbound traffic from AI search today.

**2. Do not ignore Gemini's trajectory.**
Gemini grew 647% in 12 months. Its integration with Google Search means it will shape how AI Overviews surface your content. Understanding [Google AI Overviews optimization](/blog/optimize-google-ai-overviews) is now a standard SEO skill.

**3. Fix your attribution before you scale AI search efforts.**
If 70.6% of AI traffic looks like direct traffic in GA4, your ROI calculations are wrong. Set up UTM-aware link tracking, use server logs where available, and read our post on [tracking AI search visibility](/blog/track-ai-search-visibility).

AI platforms cite the content they have seen and indexed most. Publishing consistently. Across your core topic clusters. Increases the probability of citation across all platforms. This is the [Content Compound Effect](/blog/organic-vs-ai-search-budget) in practice.

**What is the current AI search market share breakdown?**

**How much traffic do AI search platforms send to websites?**

**Why does GA4 undercount AI search traffic?**

[The Digital Bloom found](https://thedigitalbloom.com/learn/gen-ai-website-traffic-share-february-2026/) that 70.6% of AI referral sessions are misclassified as direct traffic in GA4. This happens because many AI platforms do not pass referrer headers in the standard way that analytics tools expect. Without proper UTM tracking or server-side logging, most businesses do not see their true AI search traffic numbers.

**How fast is Gemini growing compared to ChatGPT?**

Gemini grew 647% year over year. From 5.7% to 21.5% market share. While ChatGPT fell by 22 percentage points. At the current trajectory, Gemini is the only platform positioned to challenge ChatGPT's lead within the next 1–2 years. Source: [Sedestral](https://sedestral.com/en/blog/ai-search-market-share-2026).
