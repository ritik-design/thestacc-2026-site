## Why People Switch from SEO.AI

SEO.AI promises full autopilot. Plan, write, publish, repeat. On paper, it's the closest thing to a hands-off content engine. But after testing it and reading user reviews on G2, Trustpilot, and Reddit, the same problems keep coming up.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **High entry price for unproven results.** $149/month monthly ($89 annual) is steep for a tool that's still establishing itself. Competitors like Scalenut ($30/mo annual) and Koala AI ($9/mo annual) offer similar automation features at a fraction of the cost.

2. **Keyword research generates irrelevant suggestions.** Users report that SEO.AI's keyword gap tool surfaces keywords that have nothing to do with their niche. If your keyword foundation is wrong, everything built on top of it. Articles, clusters, internal links. Is wasted effort.

3. **Features don't always work.** Multiple users report that the Keyword Gap feature is non-functional. For a $149/month tool, broken core features are a dealbreaker.

4. **SEO scoring is misleading.** Keyword-stuffed gibberish can score "excellent" in SEO.AI's content grader. That means the score measures keyword density, not quality. Publishing content that scores well but reads poorly will hurt your site.

5. **Website-only. No local SEO or social.** SEO.AI focuses exclusively on blog content. No Google Business Profile posts, no social media scheduling. If you run a local business, you need separate tools and budgets for those channels.

6. **Multi-site pricing scales fast.** Single Site is $149/month. Multi Site jumps to $299/month. Agencies managing 5+ client sites face significant costs compared to alternatives with unlimited-domain plans.

## #7. Machined AI. Best for Content Clusters

Machined AI takes a different approach. Instead of individual articles, it generates entire topic clusters. Give it a seed keyword and it builds a hub page plus supporting articles with internal links.

**Key features:**

- Automated topic cluster generation from a single keyword
- Hub-and-spoke content architecture
- Automatic internal linking between cluster articles
- WordPress and Webflow auto-publish
- BYOK (bring your own API key) model for AI generation

**Pricing:** Starter $49/mo + API costs · Growth $99/mo + API · Scale $199/mo + API. Free plan available (limited clusters).

**Pros:** Only tool on this list that builds complete topic clusters automatically. Strong internal linking architecture. WordPress and Webflow publishing. Free plan to test the cluster approach.

**Cons:** BYOK model means you pay OpenAI API costs on top of the subscription. Total costs are unpredictable. AI-generated images are poor quality. Content still needs editing and human review. Smaller user base with limited community support.

**Best for:** Content marketers building topical authority through hub-and-spoke content strategies who want the cluster structure automated.

**The bottom line:** Machined AI solves a problem SEO.AI doesn't even attempt. Automated topic clustering. If your SEO strategy is built around topical authority, the cluster approach is valuable. But the BYOK pricing model makes total costs unpredictable, and content quality still requires human oversight.
