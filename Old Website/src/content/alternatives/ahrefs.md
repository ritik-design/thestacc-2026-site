## Why People Switch from Ahrefs

Ahrefs is one of the most respected SEO tools in the industry. The backlink index is unmatched. The data is trusted by professionals worldwide. But after testing it and reading hundreds of user reviews on G2, Trustpilot, and Reddit, the same complaints keep showing up.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **Expensive at full capacity.** The Starter plan is $29/month but limits you to 500 credits/month and basic features. Not enough for real work. Lite is $129/month. Standard is $249/month. Advanced is $449/month. Most users need Lite minimum, and full value lives in Standard ($249/mo).

2. **Data-focused, not action-focused.** Ahrefs tells you what keywords to target, which backlinks to pursue, and where your competitors rank. But it doesn't write a single word of content, publish anything to your website, or manage your local SEO. You get data. You still need to do everything with it.

3. **No content writing or optimization.** Ahrefs has no built-in AI writer. No content scoring like Surfer or Frase. No optimization recommendations beyond basic keyword data. For content teams, that means adding $49-$129/month in additional tools.

4. **Starter plan is heavily restricted.** The $29/month Starter plan caps you at 500 credits/month, 1 verified project, and basic features. Most serious users burn through 500 credits in the first week. It's a demo, not a plan.

5. **No local SEO or GBP management.** Ahrefs has zero Google Business Profile features. No citation management. No local rank tracking. If you run a local service business, you need a separate tool and budget.

6. **Learning curve is steep.** Ahrefs is built for SEO professionals. Small business owners who just want more organic traffic find Site Explorer, Content Explorer, and Keywords Explorer overwhelming. The power comes with complexity.
