## Why People Switch from LongShot AI

LongShot AI positions itself as the fact-checked AI writer. Its FactGPT feature claims to verify AI content against real sources. But after testing it and reading user reviews, the same problems keep showing up.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **Fact-checking is hit-or-miss.** LongShot's core differentiator. AI fact-checking. Doesn't catch all inaccuracies. It adds false confidence. You still need to verify everything manually.

2. **Credit-based pricing limits output.** Monthly word and credit limits mean you can't scale without upgrading. The credit system is complex and hard to predict.

3. **SEO optimization is basic.** SEO suggestions exist but don't match Surfer or Frase for SERP-based scoring depth. No competitive analysis against ranking pages.

4. **UI/UX is confusing.** Multiple features (FactGPT, workflows, templates, integrations) are scattered across the interface. Navigation is unintuitive, especially for new users.

5. **Smaller user base.** Less community support, fewer integrations, limited third-party documentation compared to Jasper, Surfer, or Frase.

6. **No auto-publish or local SEO.** No CMS integration for automated publishing. No Google Business Profile features. Blog content only.
