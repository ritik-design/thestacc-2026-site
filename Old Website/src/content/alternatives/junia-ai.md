## Why People Switch from Junia AI

Junia AI is an AI content writer with basic SEO features and multi-platform publishing. It's affordable and ships content fast. But after testing it and reading user reviews, the same issues keep coming up.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **SEO optimization is surface-level.** Junia generates SEO-friendly content, but it doesn't score your content against what's already ranking. No SERP analysis, no competitor-based content scoring like Surfer or Frase.

2. **AI content quality is inconsistent.** Long-form articles read as obviously AI-generated. Technical or niche topics get generic, surface-level treatment that requires heavy editing.

3. **Data loss issues.** Users report edits being lost after saving. For long articles that require manual revision, losing work is a dealbreaker.

4. **URL fetcher is unreliable.** The feature that pulls reference content from URLs frequently fails or is blocked by target websites, limiting research capabilities.

5. **Misleading free tier.** The free plan exists but is extremely limited. Users report being pushed to upgrade almost immediately.

6. **No local SEO or GBP.** Blog-only focus. No Google Business Profile posts, social media management, or local SEO features.
