## Why People Switch from Anyword

Anyword is a performance copywriting platform. Its core feature. Predictive performance scoring. Estimates how well your copy will perform before you publish. But after testing it and reading user reviews, the same limitations keep surfacing.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **Expensive for full features.** The Starter plan is $39/month, but real value is in the Data-Driven plan ($99/mo) and Business ($499/mo). Predictive scoring. Anyword's core differentiator. Is limited on lower tiers.

2. **Performance copy focus, not SEO.** Anyword excels at predicting ad and email performance. It does not optimize for search rankings. No SERP analysis, no content scoring against competitor pages.

3. **Not built for long-form.** Strong for short-form (ads, emails, social captions) but long-form blog content is secondary. Quality drops noticeably on articles over 1,000 words.

4. **No publishing or CMS integration.** Copy is generated but not published anywhere automatically. You copy-paste into your CMS, email tool, or ad platform manually.

5. **Predictive scores are directional, not precise.** The performance prediction feature is useful for A/B testing ideas, but users report predictions don't always correlate with actual results.

6. **No local SEO or GBP.** No Google Business Profile features. No local SEO. Ad copy and blog content only.
