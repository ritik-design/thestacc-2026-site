## Why People Switch from Rankability

Rankability is a content optimization platform that helps you create SEO-optimized content with AI assistance and SERP analysis. It has strong ratings from early adopters. But after testing it and reading user feedback, the same concerns keep surfacing.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **$149/month entry is expensive.** At $149/month (~$124 annual), Rankability costs more than Surfer SEO ($99/mo) and offers a narrower feature set. The price-to-value ratio is hard to justify against established competitors.

2. **Newer tool with limited market presence.** Small user base means fewer reviews, less community support, and limited third-party resources. When you hit an issue, finding help is harder.

3. **Feature set still maturing.** Core content optimization works, but the platform lacks the breadth of Surfer (500+ ranking factors), Frase (brief building), or MarketMuse (topical authority mapping).

4. **No CMS integration.** No auto-publishing to WordPress, Webflow, or any other CMS. You create content in Rankability and publish manually.

5. **No local SEO or GBP.** Blog optimization only. No Google Business Profile posts, social media, or local SEO features.

6. **Overkill for small businesses.** The technical depth is useful for SEO practitioners, but small business owners who just want content published don't need this level of optimization data.
