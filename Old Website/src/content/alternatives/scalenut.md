## Why People Switch from Scalenut

Scalenut is one of the most feature-packed content tools at its price point. AI writing, content optimization, GEO features, backlinks marketplace. All for $30/month on annual billing. But after testing it and reading hundreds of user reviews on G2, Trustpilot, and Reddit, the same complaints keep showing up.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **Feature set spread thin.** Scalenut tries to do everything. Content optimization, AI writing, GEO, backlinks marketplace, social upreach. But none of it at the depth of specialized tools. Jack of all trades, master of none.

2. **AI content quality needs heavy editing.** Generated articles are functional but generic and robotic. Technical or niche topics require significant rewriting. Multiple users report the output sounds like every other AI tool.

3. **Cancellation nightmares.** This is the most common complaint across review sites. Users report extreme difficulty cancelling subscriptions, being charged after cancellation requests, and support tickets going unanswered for weeks.

4. **Content optimization scoring is less refined.** Users who need precise, granular content scoring find Scalenut's optimizer less reliable than Surfer or Clearscope. The scoring engine works but lacks depth.

5. **WordPress-only publishing.** Auto-publish works with WordPress only. No Webflow, Ghost, Shopify, or other CMS integrations. If your site isn't on WordPress, you're copying and pasting.

6. **No local SEO or GBP.** Despite marketing to small businesses, Scalenut has zero Google Business Profile features. Local service businesses need a separate tool for local search.
