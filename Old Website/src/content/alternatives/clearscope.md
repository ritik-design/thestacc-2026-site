## Why People Switch from Clearscope

Clearscope is the gold standard for content grading. Its A++ scoring system is trusted by Conde Nast, Shopify, and HubSpot. But after testing it and reading hundreds of user reviews on G2, Trustpilot, and Reddit, the same complaints surface repeatedly.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **Expensive with no AI writer.** $129/month for Essentials. And it does not write content. You pay for optimization scoring only. Add writers ($80-$250/article) and the real cost is $2,500+/month for 30 articles.

2. **No free trial.** Clearscope requires you to commit blind. No trial, no free plan, no money-back guarantee. Every competitor on this list offers some way to test before paying.

3. **Business plan jumps to $399/month.** If your team outgrows 10 reports on Essentials, the next tier is a steep jump. Adding extra reports costs $60-$100 per batch.

4. **Feature-light for the price.** At $129/month, competitors like Frase ($49), Scalenut ($30 annual), and NeuronWriter ($23) offer more features for less. Clearscope does one thing well. But only one thing.

5. **No auto-publish.** Integrates with Google Docs and WordPress for editing, but there is no automated publishing workflow. You still format and publish manually.

6. **No local SEO or GBP.** Zero Google Business Profile features. If you run a local service business, you need a separate tool and budget for local search.
