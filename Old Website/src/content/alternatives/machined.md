## Why People Switch from Machined AI

Machined AI is built for one thing: generating interconnected content clusters at scale. It creates topic clusters with internal linking automatically. But after testing it and reading user feedback, the same concerns keep surfacing.

**The most common reasons people switch:**

1. **Content cluster focus is narrow.** Machined generates interconnected article clusters. If you need single articles, content briefs, or optimization scoring, it's not the right tool.

2. **AI content quality needs work.** Bulk-generated cluster articles tend toward generic, formulaic output. Each article reads like a variation of the others. Heavy editing is required for uniqueness and value.

3. **No SERP-based optimization.** No content scoring against competitor pages like Surfer or Frase. Articles are generated from AI models, not optimized against what's already ranking.

4. **AI-generated images are poor quality.** Users report that auto-generated feature images look obviously artificial and need to be replaced manually.

5. **Newer tool with limited track record.** Small user base, fewer reviews, and a smaller support ecosystem than established alternatives.

6. **No local SEO, social, or GBP.** Blog content clusters only. No Google Business Profile posts, social media, or local SEO features.
