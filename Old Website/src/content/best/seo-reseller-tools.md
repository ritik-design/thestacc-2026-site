## Frequently Asked Questions

**What are SEO reseller tools?**

Others are white-label platforms that give you branded dashboards and reports. You buy at wholesale. Sell at retail. Keep the margin.

**What's the difference between an SEO reseller tool and a white-label tool?**
Overlap is significant. White-label means the client sees your brand, not the tool's. Reseller specifically means you're buying a service at one price and selling it at a higher price. Most tools on this list are both. white-labeled and resellable.

**What margins should I expect from SEO reselling?**
Tool-based reselling (content, reporting) delivers 80-93% margins. Managed service reselling (SEOReseller, Boostability) delivers 50-67%. The difference: tools automate fulfillment. Managed services use human labor you're paying for.

**How many clients do I need to make SEO reselling profitable?**
One client covers most tool costs. theStacc at $99/month is profitable from client one if you charge $500+/month. Vendasta at $399/month needs 3-4 clients to break even. The break-even point depends on your tool stack and pricing.

**Can clients find out I'm reselling?**
Not with proper white-labeling. Reports show your logo. Content publishes from your workflow. Dashboards use your domain. The client sees results from your agency. not your tool stack.

**What's the best starting stack for a new SEO reseller?**
theStacc ($99/month for content) + BrightLocal ($39/month for local tracking) + AgencyAnalytics ($12/client for reporting). Total: ~$150/client. Sell at $1,500/month minimum. That's a 90% margin from day one.

**Do I need technical SEO skills to resell?**
No. Managed services like SEOReseller and Boostability handle strategy and execution. Content services like theStacc handle writing and publishing. Reporting tools like AgencyAnalytics handle analytics. Your job is sales and client management.

**Is it ethical to resell SEO services?**
Yes. Every agency uses tools and contractors. Reselling is the same model. you're the account manager, strategist, and client contact. The tools and services handle execution. Your value is the relationship, the strategy, and the accountability.
