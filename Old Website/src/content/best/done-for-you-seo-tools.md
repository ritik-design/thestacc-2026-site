## 2. Search Atlas (OTTO SEO). Best for Full-Stack SEO Automation

The deepest SEO automation platform on the market. OTTO handles technical SEO fixes, content creation, backlink building via digital PR, and GBP management. all through a single dashboard.

### What It Does Well

OTTO is the closest thing to a full-time SEO team in software form. Install a JavaScript tag snippet on your site, and the OTTO agent auto-fixes technical SEO issues: meta tags, schema markup, broken links, canonicals, header optimization, alt text, and internal linking. It generates content from topical maps, builds backlinks through press releases and digital PR outreach, and manages Google Business Profile with heatmap-based local ranking data.

The breadth is unmatched. No other tool covers technical fixes + content + backlinks + local SEO in one platform.

> **Our Take:** OTTO is impressive in scope, but scope creates complexity. Small business owners won't need 80% of what OTTO offers. This is an agency-grade tool built for teams managing multiple client sites, not a solopreneur wanting hands-off blog content.

### Where It Falls Short

The GBP module ("Galactic") has been reported as buggy by users on G2, with some describing it as "perpetually broken." The platform has reported crashes and slow report loading. Support offers no phone line, and video call wait times average 4 days. The real cost can stack up. $99/month base plus $59-99/site/month for OTTO add-ons means you're looking at $158-198/month per site minimum.

### Key Features

- Technical SEO auto-fixes (meta tags, schema, broken links, canonicals)
- AI content creation from topical maps
- Backlink building via digital PR and press releases
- Local SEO/GBP management with ranking heatmaps
- Works on any CMS via JavaScript tag snippet
- WordPress, Shopify, and Webflow direct integrations

### Pricing

- **Starter:** $99/month (1 OTTO project, 2 seats)
- **Growth:** $199/month (4 OTTO projects)
- **Pro:** $399/month (8 OTTO projects)
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing
- **OTTO add-on:** $59-99/site/month on top of base plan
- 20% annual discount. 7-day free trial (credit card required).

### Who Should Use Search Atlas

**Strong fit:**
- SEO agencies managing 5+ client websites
- Mid-size businesses with $200-400/month SEO budgets wanting full-stack automation

**Probably not right for:**
- Solo business owners (too complex, too expensive for a single site)
- Anyone needing reliable GBP management (the module needs work)

30 auto-published articles for $99/month. $3.30 per article. with the widest CMS support of any autopilot tool. [Read our full Outrank review](/reviews/outrank/) for the complete analysis.

### Key Features

- 30 articles/month auto-published to your CMS

- Volume discounts for multi-site operators
- $1 for 3-day trial

### Pricing

### Who Should Use Outrank
