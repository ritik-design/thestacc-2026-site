## What Makes Blog Content "SEO Rich"?

You wrote 30 blog posts last quarter. Traffic didn't move. Rankings stayed flat. The content reads fine. But Google doesn't care about "reads fine."

SEO-rich blog content hits specific markers. Keyword density in the right range. Semantic variations that signal topical depth. Header structures that match search intent. Internal links that distribute authority. Meta descriptions that earn clicks. NLP terms that Google's algorithms expect to see on a page about your topic.

Most AI writing tools generate text. They don't generate SEO-rich text. The difference matters. An article that scores 45 on content optimization will sit on page 7. The same article rewritten to score 85+ competes for page 1. That gap between "written" and "optimized" is where most AI tools fail.

We tested 10 AI tools with one question: **Does the output score high enough on SEO metrics to rank without manual optimization?**

We measured keyword targeting, semantic coverage, content scores, header structure, and internal linking. We ran every output through Surfer SEO's Content Score and checked NLP term coverage. Below. honest results, real pricing, and who each tool actually serves.
