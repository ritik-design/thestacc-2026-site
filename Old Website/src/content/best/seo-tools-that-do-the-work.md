## Frequently Asked Questions

**What does it mean for an SEO tool to "do the work for you"?**

It means the tool handles keyword research, content writing, optimization, and publishing without manual input after initial setup. You connect your CMS, configure your preferences, and the tool runs on autopilot. The level of automation varies, some tools are truly hands-off (Stacc, Emplibot, RankYak), while others produce drafts needing 20-60% editing (Scalenut, RightBlogger, Autoblogging.ai).

**Can automated SEO content actually rank on Google?**

Yes. Research from Ahrefs analyzing thousands of top-ranking pages shows AI-generated content is present in a significant percentage of top-10 Google results. Google's stance is that content quality matters more than how it was produced. Tools with NLP optimization and topical depth perform best. thin, keyword-stuffed output will not rank regardless of who or what created it.

**How many articles per month do you need for SEO results?**

Data from HubSpot shows businesses publishing 16+ articles per month get 3.5x more traffic than those publishing 0-4. For most small businesses, 10-30 articles per month is the sweet spot for building topical authority. The exact number depends on your industry's competitiveness and the depth of your current content library.

**Are these tools cheaper than hiring an SEO agency?**

Significantly. SEO agencies typically charge $3,000-10,000 per month for 4-8 articles plus strategy. Autopilot tools range from $19-499 per month with equal or greater output volume. Even Adaptify at $499/month, the most expensive tool on this list, is 50-90% cheaper than a mid-tier agency.

**Do I still need to review automated content before publishing?**

It depends on the tool. Fully automated tools (Stacc, Emplibot, RankYak) publish without requiring review. Semi-automated tools (Scalenut, RightBlogger) produce drafts needing significant editing. We recommend spot-checking even fully automated tools monthly to verify factual accuracy and brand voice alignment.

**Which SEO tool that does the work is best for local businesses?**

theStacc is the only service on this list combining blog content automation with Google Business Profile post automation and social media. Local businesses need visibility on Google Maps (GBP), in organic search (blog content), and on social media. **What is the difference between an autopilot SEO tool and an AI writing tool?**

An AI writing tool (like Jasper or Writesonic) helps you write content faster, you still handle keyword research, optimization, formatting, and publishing yourself. An autopilot tool handles the entire pipeline from keyword selection to published article without your involvement. Every tool on this list is in the autopilot category, though some (Scalenut, RightBlogger) lean closer to the writing-tool side.
