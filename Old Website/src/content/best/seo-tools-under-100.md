The listing score measures your online presence health. It shows how complete and consistent your information is across directories. A low score means customers on different platforms find conflicting data.

> **Our Take:** Moz Local is the cheapest way to ensure your business information is correct everywhere. At $20/month, it costs less than a single hour of manually updating listings across 90 directories. If customers have ever found wrong hours, an old phone number, or a previous address for your business online, Moz Local pays for itself in the first month.
