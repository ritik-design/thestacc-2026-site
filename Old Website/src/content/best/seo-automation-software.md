## What We Mean by "SEO Automation"

Most businesses publish 1-4 blog posts per month. The businesses ranking on page one publish 20-30. The gap isn't talent or strategy. It's capacity.

SEO automation closes that gap. But the term covers everything from auto-generating meta descriptions to a full AI agent managing your entire content pipeline. Not all automation is equal, and not all of it saves time.

We tested 15 tools across 4 categories of automation:

- **Content automation**: keyword research, writing, optimization, publishing
- **Technical automation**, site audits, meta tags, schema, broken links, canonicals
- **Tracking automation**, rank tracking, reporting, alerts
- **Full-stack automation**, all of the above in a single platform

We evaluated each tool on 5 criteria:

| Criteria | What We Measured |
|---|---|
| **Automation Level** | How much runs without human intervention after setup? |
| **Content Quality** | Would you publish the output without editing? |
| **Pricing Transparency** | Is the real cost clear, or are there hidden add-ons? |
| **CMS Support** | WordPress only, or multi-platform? |
| **What's Missing** | What will you still need another tool or service for? |

Below is an honest breakdown of each tool. What it automates, what it doesn't, the real cost, and who it actually serves.

## 4. Alli AI. Best Technical SEO Automation

Automated on-page SEO fixes across your entire site. Meta tags, schema, headers, broken links, canonicals. all managed from one dashboard without touching your CMS code.

### What It Automates

Alli AI adds a JavaScript snippet to your site and applies technical SEO changes across every page without modifying source code. Changes render through the snippet, so you can roll back anything instantly.

Bulk meta tag optimization rewrites titles and descriptions across thousands of pages. Schema markup generation adds structured data (FAQ, LocalBusiness, Product, Article). Header tag restructuring fixes H1/H2/H3 hierarchy site-wide. Internal linking connects orphaned pages. Canonical tag management prevents duplicates. Alt text generation covers images missing descriptions.

For sites with 500+ pages of accumulated technical debt, Alli fixes in hours what would take a specialist weeks.

> **Our Take:** Alli AI is the best option for technical SEO automation. If your site has hundreds of pages with missing meta tags, broken schema, or canonical issues, Alli fixes them at scale without a developer touching the codebase. The JS snippet approach means changes are reversible and don't require CMS access. But at $249/month minimum for 5 sites, it's expensive for a single-purpose tool. And it does zero content creation.

### Where It Falls Short

No content creation, no blog publishing, no keyword research, no GBP, no backlinks, no rank tracking.

$249-299/month for 5 sites is steep for a single-function tool. Additional sites cost $39 each. 10-day trial is shorter than most competitors. Works with WordPress, Shopify, and custom sites only. no native Webflow, Wix, or Squarespace support. Changes apply via JS overlay, not hard-coded modifications.
