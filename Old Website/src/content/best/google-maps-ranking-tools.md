## 1. Local Falcon. Best Google Maps Ranking Tool for Geo-Grid Tracking

Local Falcon is the standard for visualizing your Google Maps rankings across a geographic grid. If you spend money on local SEO and cannot see whether it works block by block, you are guessing. Local Falcon ends the guessing.

### What It Does Well

You enter your business address, choose a keyword, set a scan radius from 1 to 20 miles, and Local Falcon checks your Maps position at dozens of grid points. The result is a color-coded heatmap. Green means top 3. Yellow means positions 4-10. Red means you are buried.

This geographic detail changes how you think about local SEO. You might rank #1 within 2 miles but drop to #12 at 4 miles. That tells you exactly where your visibility falls off. and where to focus. Run scans monthly and you can see whether your content, citations, and GBP activity are expanding your ranking radius over time.

Competitor scanning runs the same grid analysis on any business. Overlay your heatmap against a competitor's and see precisely where they outrank you. Trend tracking stores historical scans so you can measure progress over weeks and months.

> **Our Take:** Local Falcon answers the question every local business should ask: "Where exactly do I show up in Maps?" One scan per month costs about 50 credits. That single data point tells you more about your local SEO performance than any dashboard metric. For $24.99/month, it is the cheapest way to prove whether your local SEO investment is working.

### Where It Falls Short

Local Falcon is tracking only. It tells you where you rank. it does not help you rank higher. No content creation, no GBP post scheduling, no citation management, no review tools. Credit-based pricing means heavy scanning gets expensive. A 15x15 grid uses 225 credits per scan. Agencies tracking 10+ locations across multiple keywords burn through credits fast.

**What is geo-grid rank tracking?**
Geo-grid tracking checks your Google Maps ranking from dozens of geographic points across a grid.
