## What Your Agency Actually Delivers (for $3,000-10,000/Month)

Before comparing alternatives, you need to understand what you're replacing. Most mid-tier SEO agencies deliver:

- **4-8 blog articles per month** (800-1,500 words, outsourced to freelancers at $80-250/article, marked up 3-5x)
- **Monthly or quarterly site audit** with basic fixes (broken links, redirects, missing meta descriptions)
- **Monthly performance report** (largely automated Google Analytics + Search Console screenshots)
- **Quarterly strategy call** (30-60 minutes with an account manager handling 10-20 other clients)
- **Keyword tracking** updates

That's the standard package. Content takes 2-3 weeks from brief to publication. Technical work gets front-loaded in months 1-3, then drops to occasional maintenance.

What's notably missing at the $3,000-5,000 tier: Google Business Profile management, social media posting, meaningful backlink building, landing page content, and paid media. Link building, if included, is typically low-quality guest posts or directory submissions.

## When You Should Keep Your Agency

Automation tools handle execution. Agencies handle judgment. The distinction matters. Here's when keeping your agency is the right call:

### You Need Custom Strategy

Competitive keyword targeting, content positioning, and brand narrative require human judgment. Tools publish 30 articles a month, but can't decide whether to pivot toward a new audience segment or reposition against an emerging competitor.

### You're in a Regulated Industry

Healthcare, finance, legal, and pharmaceutical content carries compliance risks. HIPAA, SEC regulations, bar association rules, and FDA guidelines require human review. One factual error in a medical article creates genuine liability. Agencies with industry compliance expertise are worth the premium.

### You Need Creative Campaigns

PR stunts, influencer partnerships, brand storytelling, viral content strategies, these require human creativity and relationship management. No tool builds journalist relationships or creates campaign concepts that generate earned media.

### Your Site Has Complex Technical Issues

Site migrations, internationalization (hreflang), custom schema, Core Web Vitals optimization across thousands of pages, JavaScript rendering issues. Automated audits flag issues but can't implement fixes on complex custom-built sites.

### You Value a Dedicated Account Manager

Some businesses need a human who knows their business, answers the phone, and proactively suggests changes. Automation tools provide dashboards. Agencies provide people.
