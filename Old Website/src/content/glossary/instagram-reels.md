## What Are Instagram Reels?

Instagram Reels are short-form, vertical videos (9:16 aspect ratio) lasting up to 90 seconds that Instagram distributes to both followers and non-followers through its recommendation algorithm.

Meta launched Reels in August 2020 as a direct response to [TikTok](/glossary/tiktok-seo)'s explosive growth. The format quickly became Instagram's fastest-growing feature, and by 2024, Reels accounted for over 50% of time spent on the platform according to Meta's earnings calls. Unlike traditional Instagram posts that primarily reach your existing followers, Reels get pushed to new audiences through the Reels tab and Explore page.

That distribution model is the key differentiator. A business account with 500 followers can get 50,000 views on a single Reel if the content resonates. Instagram's head Adam Mosseri has repeatedly stated that Reels are the best way to grow reach on the platform.

## Why Do Instagram Reels Matter?

Reels are Instagram's primary growth and discovery mechanism. If you're not creating Reels, you're leaving the biggest reach opportunity on the platform untouched.

- **Highest organic reach of any Instagram format**. Reels reach 2-3x more non-followers than static posts or carousels, according to Hootsuite's 2024 Social Trends report
- **Algorithm priority**. Instagram actively boosts Reels in the main feed, Explore page, and dedicated Reels tab. Meta has been transparent about prioritizing video.
- **Cross-platform distribution**. Reels can be shared to Facebook Reels automatically, doubling your distribution with zero extra work
- **Purchase influence** ,  44% of Instagram users shop weekly on the platform, and Reels with product tags drive direct [social commerce](/glossary/social-commerce) conversions
- **SMB equalizer**. The algorithm cares about content quality, not account size. Small businesses compete on the same playing field as major brands.

For local businesses and SMBs, Reels represent the cheapest way to reach thousands of potential customers without spending a dollar on ads.

## How Instagram Reels Work

### Creation

You record or upload [vertical video](/glossary/vertical-video) through the Instagram app (or schedule via Meta Business Suite). Reels support music from Instagram's library, voiceover, text overlays, AR effects, and basic editing features like trim, speed adjustment, and align (for seamless transitions).

### Distribution Algorithm

Instagram's Reels algorithm evaluates content based on four primary signals:

1. **User activity**. What Reels has this user liked, shared, saved, and watched fully before?
2. **Interaction history**. Has this user engaged with your account previously?
3. **Reel information**. Audio track, video content (Instagram's AI analyzes the actual frames), caption text, and hashtags
4. **Creator information**. Your overall [engagement rate](/glossary/engagement-rate), posting frequency, and content history

The algorithm serves your Reel to a small test audience first. If they watch it fully, share it, or save it, Instagram expands distribution. Poor initial performance means limited reach. Watch time is the single most important metric.

### Monetization

Creators can earn through Instagram's bonus programs (invitation-only), brand partnerships, and product tagging via [Instagram Shopping](/glossary/instagram-shopping). For businesses, the real monetization isn't direct payouts. It's the leads and sales generated by reaching new audiences.

## Types of Instagram Reels

Different Reel formats serve different marketing goals:

- **Educational Reels**. Quick tips, how-tos, or myth-busting. These perform well for service businesses. A dentist explaining "3 signs you need a root canal" or a realtor walking through "What to expect at closing."
- **Behind-the-scenes Reels**. Showing your process, workspace, or team. Builds trust and humanizes the business. [Behind-the-scenes content](/glossary/behind-the-scenes-content) consistently gets high saves.
- **Trending audio Reels**. Using [trending sounds](/glossary/trending-sound) or music to ride algorithmic waves. These get boosted by Instagram when a sound is peaking. Timing matters. Jump on trends within 48 hours.
- **Testimonial and result Reels**. Before/after transformations, customer stories, or project showcases. High [social proof](/glossary/social-proof) value. These convert followers into customers.
- **Collab Reels**. Co-created with another account, appearing on both profiles simultaneously. Doubles reach instantly.

## Instagram Reels vs. TikTok

These formats compete directly, and businesses often wonder which one to prioritize.

| | Instagram Reels | TikTok |
|---|---|---|
| **Max length** | 90 seconds | 10 minutes |
| **Primary audience (US)** | 25-44 age range | 16-34 age range |
| **Discovery algorithm** | Strong but secondary to follow graph | Pure discovery. Follow graph barely matters |
| **Shopping integration** | Instagram Shopping + product tags | [TikTok Shop](/glossary/tiktok-shop) |
| **Business tools** | Meta Business Suite, scheduling, analytics | TikTok Business Center |
| **Cross-posting** | Shares to Facebook Reels automatically | Standalone platform |
| **SEO value** | Low. Instagram search is limited | Growing ,  [TikTok SEO](/glossary/tiktok-seo) is emerging as a search engine |

Most businesses should post on both. The content creation effort is the same. You're making vertical videos either way.

## Instagram Reels Best Practices

- **Hook viewers in the first 1-2 seconds**. If someone scrolls past your opening, watch time tanks and the algorithm buries the Reel. Start with movement, a question, or a surprising statement. Never start with a logo intro.
- **Optimize for saves and shares, not just likes**. The algorithm weights saves and shares higher than likes. Create content people want to reference later (tips, checklists, tutorials) or share with friends.
- **Post 3-5 Reels per week**. Consistency trains the algorithm. One viral Reel per month matters less than steady output. Accounts posting 4+ Reels weekly see 2x the reach of those posting once a week.
- **Use 3-5 relevant hashtags**. Don't stuff 30 hashtags. Instagram's recommendation system relies more on content analysis than [hashtags](/glossary/hashtag) now, but a few targeted ones still help categorization. Use a [hashtag strategy](/glossary/hashtag-strategy) focused on your niche.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the ideal length for Instagram Reels?

Reels between 7-15 seconds tend to get the highest completion rates, which drives algorithmic distribution. However, educational content can perform well at 30-60 seconds if retention stays high.

### Do Instagram Reels help with SEO?

Not directly. Reels don't rank in Google Search. But they drive brand awareness, website clicks, and social signals that indirectly support your [organic traffic](/glossary/organic-traffic) and brand search volume.

### Can I repost TikToks as Reels?

Technically yes, but remove the TikTok watermark first. Instagram has confirmed that watermarked content from other platforms gets deprioritized by the algorithm.

### Should businesses use trending audio?

Yes, when it fits your brand. Reels with [trending audio](/glossary/trending-audio) get an algorithmic boost during the trend's peak. But forced trend participation looks awkward. Skip trends that don't connect to your message.
