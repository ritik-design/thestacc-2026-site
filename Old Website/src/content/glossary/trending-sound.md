## What is a Trending Sound?

A trending sound is an audio track that's being widely used across TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts. And the platform's algorithm is actively promoting content that features it.

Trending sounds can be song clips, movie dialogue, original voiceovers, or sound effects that go viral. When you use a trending sound in your video, the algorithm is more likely to surface your content to a broader audience. It's the audio equivalent of riding a wave. The platform wants that sound to spread, and it rewards creators who help it spread.

TikTok's own data shows that videos using trending sounds see up to 120% more views than those with original or non-trending audio. That's not a minor bump. It's the difference between 500 views and 5,000.

## Why Do Trending Sounds Matter?

Sounds drive the algorithm. Ignoring them means leaving [organic reach](/glossary/organic-reach) on the table.

- **Algorithm boost**. Platforms prioritize content using sounds that are currently popular. Using them is like getting a free visibility push
- **Cultural relevance**. Trending sounds connect your content to a broader conversation. Your audience recognizes the sound and is more likely to stop scrolling
- **Lower production bar**. You don't need original music or professional audio. The sound does the heavy lifting. Your job is the visual hook
- **[Virality rate](/glossary/virality-rate) increases**. Users are more likely to share content set to familiar, trending audio

Brands and [content creators](/glossary/content-creator) who consistently use trending sounds grow faster than those who don't.

## How Trending Sounds Work

### Discovery

Open TikTok's Creative Center or check Instagram's Reels tab for a "trending audio" indicator (upward arrow icon). Third-party tools like TrendTok and Tokboard also track rising sounds before they peak.

### Timing

Trending sounds have a lifecycle. Typically 3-14 days of peak popularity. Jump on early and you ride the upswing. Jump on late and you're competing with millions of other videos using the same audio. Speed matters here.

### Adaptation

The best-performing brands adapt trending sounds to their niche. A dental practice lip-syncing a trending audio about "the glow-up" to show before-and-after smiles. An accounting firm using a dramatic sound over tax deadline countdowns. Relevance plus trend equals reach.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do you find trending sounds?

On TikTok, check the Creative Center or the "For You" page for recurring audio. On Instagram, look for the upward arrow icon next to audio names in Reels. Third-party tools like TrendTok provide early alerts.

### Can businesses use trending sounds?

Yes, but some sounds have copyright restrictions for business accounts. Instagram and TikTok both have commercial music libraries. Always check the sound's availability for business use before posting.

### How quickly should you use a trending sound?

Within the first 3-5 days of the sound gaining traction. After that, the algorithm starts deprioritizing it as the market gets saturated with content using the same audio.
