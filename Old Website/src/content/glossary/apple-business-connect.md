## What is Apple Business Connect?

Apple Business Connect is Apple's business listing management platform. The Apple Maps equivalent of [Google Business Profile](/glossary/google-business-profile). That lets businesses claim and customize how they appear across Apple Maps, Siri, Wallet, Safari, and other Apple products.

Launched in January 2023, it replaced the older Apple Maps Connect. The platform gives businesses control over their location cards. The information users see when they search for a business on any Apple device. With over 1 billion active Apple devices worldwide, this isn't a niche platform.

Apple Maps usage has grown significantly since Apple replaced Google Maps as the default on iPhones. Internal Apple data suggests that Apple Maps handles over 5 billion requests per week. For local businesses, ignoring Apple Business Connect means being invisible to a significant share of mobile searchers.

## Why Does Apple Business Connect Matter?

Apple devices represent a massive, often overlooked audience for local businesses.

- **iPhone dominance**. In the US, iPhones hold 57% market share. Apple Maps is the default for Siri, Safari, and native apps
- **Siri integration** ,  "Hey Siri, find a dentist near me" pulls from Apple Maps data. Claimed businesses with complete profiles rank better
- **[Citation signal](/glossary/citation-building)**. An active Apple Business Connect listing serves as a high-authority citation that Google can also detect and validate
- **Showcase features**. Apple allows custom action buttons, special offers, photos, and branded visuals that make listings stand out

Not claiming your Apple Business Connect profile means Apple controls your listing based on third-party data. Which may be wrong.

## How Apple Business Connect Works

### Claiming Your Listing

Visit business.apple.com and sign in with an Apple ID. Search for your business, claim it, and verify ownership. Apple supports verification through phone, email, or document upload. Multi-location businesses can claim all locations through a single account.

### Customizing Your Profile

Add photos, logos, hours, services, and a branded header image. Set up action buttons (order food, book appointment, reserve table). Add custom offers and promotions. Update hours for holidays. Apple Business Connect also lets you customize the place card that appears when someone searches your business name on any Apple device.

### Keeping It Updated

Like [Google Business Profile](/glossary/google-business-profile), Apple Business Connect needs regular maintenance. Update hours for holidays, add seasonal promotions, refresh photos quarterly. Active listings appear more prominently than stale ones in Apple Maps search results.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Apple Business Connect free?

Yes, completely free. Claiming, customizing, and managing your listing costs nothing. There's no paid tier or advertising component within Business Connect itself (unlike Google, which integrates paid ads with GBP).

### Does Apple Business Connect affect Google rankings?

Not directly. But having a consistent, claimed listing on Apple Business Connect serves as a citation signal that reinforces your business's legitimacy across the broader [local search ecosystem](/glossary/local-search-ecosystem). It also captures traffic that would otherwise be invisible to businesses focused only on Google.

### Should every local business claim Apple Business Connect?

Yes. It takes 15 minutes to claim and set up. Given the size of the Apple ecosystem, leaving your Apple Maps listing unclaimed means losing potential customers who search on iPhones, iPads, and Macs. There's no reason not to do it.
