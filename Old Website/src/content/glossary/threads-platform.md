## What is Threads?

Threads is Meta's text-first social platform, launched in July 2023 as a companion to Instagram, where users share short posts, links, and images in a public conversation format.

Your Threads account is connected to your Instagram profile. Same username, same followers (if they choose to follow you on Threads). The platform focuses on text-based discussions rather than photos or videos, making it a direct competitor to X. Posts can be up to 500 characters, with images, videos, and links supported.

Threads hit 175 million monthly active users by late 2024, according to Meta's earnings reports. That growth happened without an ad platform. Meaning organic reach is currently high and competition for attention is relatively low.

## Why Does Threads Matter?

Early-mover advantage is real. Threads' organic reach is unusually high right now.

- **High organic visibility**. Without a mature ad platform, Threads isn't suppressing organic content to sell ads. Early adopters report reach levels that feel like Instagram circa 2016
- **Instagram integration**. Your existing Instagram audience can follow you with one tap. No audience-building from zero
- **Text-based branding**. For brands with strong [brand voice](/glossary/brand-voice), Threads offers a platform where writing matters more than production value
- **Low competition**. Most brands haven't developed a Threads strategy yet. Publishing consistently now builds a head start

Threads is worth testing for any brand already active on Instagram.

## How Threads Works

### Account Setup

Download the Threads app and log in with your Instagram account. Your username, name, and bio carry over. Choose whether to auto-follow everyone you follow on Instagram or start fresh.

### Content Creation

Posts support text (500 characters), images, videos (5 minutes), and links. Threads doesn't support hashtags the same way Instagram does. Discovery comes from the algorithm and reposts rather than tag-based browsing.

### Engagement and Growth

Threads rewards conversation. Replies, quotes, and reposts signal to the algorithm that your content is worth distributing. Posting 3-5 times daily and actively replying to others' posts builds visibility quickly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Threads worth it for businesses?

Yes, especially if you already have an Instagram following. The effort is low (text posts take minutes), organic reach is high, and early presence builds authority before the platform gets crowded.

### How is Threads different from X?

Threads is connected to Instagram, has no ads (yet), and leans toward positive community guidelines. X is more real-time news-focused with a mature ad platform. Threads feels more like a social conversation; X feels more like a public forum.

### Can you run ads on Threads?

Not yet. Meta has signaled that advertising is coming, but as of early 2026, Threads remains ad-free. Which is exactly why organic reach is so strong right now.
