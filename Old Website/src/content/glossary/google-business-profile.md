## What is Google Business Profile?

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is a free platform from Google that lets businesses create and manage their online presence across Google Search and Google Maps. When someone searches for your business or a service you offer, your GBP listing determines what information appears in the Local Pack, Knowledge Panel, and Maps results.

GBP is the single most important platform for local SEO. It directly controls how over 5 billion daily Google searches see your business information.

## Why Does Google Business Profile Matter?

GBP is the foundation of local search visibility:

- **Businesses with complete GBP listings** are 70% more likely to attract location visits
- **56% of actions** on GBP listings are website visits
- **Businesses with photos** receive 42% more direction requests and 35% more website clicks
- Your GBP listing is often the **first thing customers see**. Before your website

For any business serving local customers, GBP is not optional. It's the front door to your local online presence.

## Key GBP Features

| Feature | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Business Information** | Name, address, phone, hours, website URL, description |
| **Categories** | Primary and secondary business categories that determine which searches you appear for |
| **Reviews** | Customer ratings and written reviews displayed on your listing |
| **Photos & Videos** | Visual content showcasing your business, products, and team |
| **Posts** | Short updates (offers, events, news) published directly to your listing |
| **Products & Services** | Catalogs of what you offer with descriptions and pricing |
| **Messaging** | Direct customer communication through your listing |
| **Q&A** | Questions and answers visible on your listing |
| **Insights/Performance** | Data on how customers find and interact with your listing |
| **Attributes** | Feature tags (wheelchair accessible, free Wi-Fi, women-owned, etc.) |

## How to Set Up Google Business Profile

1. **Go to business.google.com** and sign in with your Google account
2. **Enter your business name**. If it exists, claim it; if not, create a new listing
3. **Select your business category**. Choose the most specific primary category available
4. **Add your location**. Enter your address or set a service area if you don't have a storefront
5. **Add contact information**. Phone number and website URL
6. **Verify your business**. Google will verify via video recording, phone call, email, or postcard
7. **Complete your profile**. Add hours, photos, description, services, and attributes

## GBP Optimization Best Practices

### Choose the Right Categories
Your primary category is the single most influential GBP ranking factor. Select the most specific option that accurately describes your business. Add 3-5 relevant secondary categories.

### Upload Quality Photos
Add at least 10 high-quality photos including your storefront, interior, team, and products/services. Businesses with 100+ photos get 520% more calls than the average listing.

### Write a Complete Description
Use all 750 characters. Include your primary services, service area, and what makes you different. Use natural language. Keyword stuffing hurts more than it helps.

### Respond to Every Review
Respond to all reviews. Positive and negative. Within 24 hours. Review response rate and quality are ranking signals. Keep responses professional and personalized.

### Post Regularly
Publish GBP posts weekly with offers, updates, or helpful content. Regular posting signals to Google that your business is active and engaged.

## GBP vs. Google My Business

Google Business Profile is the current name for what was previously called Google My Business (GMB). Google rebranded the platform in November 2021 and moved management from a separate app to directly within Google Search and Maps. The functionality is the same. The name changed.
### Local vs National SEO

| Factor | Local SEO | National SEO |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary goal** | Map Pack + local organic | Organic rankings nationally |
| **Key platform** | Google Business Profile | Website content |
| **Ranking signals** | Proximity, reviews, NAP | Backlinks, content, authority |
| **Content focus** | Location pages, local topics | Industry-wide topics |
| **Timeline** | 3-6 months | 6-12 months |
| **Competition** | Local businesses | National brands |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Google Business Profile free?

Yes, GBP is completely free to create and manage. There is no paid tier or premium version for the listing itself. Google does offer paid Local Services Ads that appear above GBP listings.

### Can I have a GBP without a physical address?

Yes. Service area businesses (SABs) like plumbers, cleaners, or mobile services can create a GBP listing by defining a service area instead of displaying a physical address.

### How long does GBP verification take?

Verification methods vary. Video verification (the most common method in 2026) can be completed within days. Phone and email verification are often instant. Postcard verification takes 5-14 days.
