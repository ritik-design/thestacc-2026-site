## What is LinkedIn Creator Mode?

LinkedIn Creator Mode is a profile feature that shifts your LinkedIn presence from networking-focused to content-focused, prioritizing your posts and granting access to creator-specific tools.

When you turn on Creator Mode, your profile changes: the "Connect" button becomes "Follow," your featured content moves to the top, and you can add up to 5 topic hashtags that signal what you create about. You also unlock access to [LinkedIn newsletters](/glossary/linkedin-newsletter), LinkedIn Live, and LinkedIn Audio Events.

LinkedIn reports that Creator Mode users see an average 30% increase in profile views and content reach. For professionals and brands building [thought leadership](/glossary/thought-leadership), it's a must-have setting.

## Why Does LinkedIn Creator Mode Matter?

LinkedIn's algorithm favors creators. This mode tells the platform you're one of them.

- **More reach per post**. Creator Mode profiles get algorithmic preference in the feed. Your content shows up more often
- **Newsletter access**. LinkedIn newsletters notify every subscriber via email and push notification. Open rates are dramatically higher than traditional email newsletters
- **"Follow" over "Connect"**. Followers see your content without you needing to accept connection requests. It builds an audience, not just a contact list
- **Professional authority**. The creator layout puts your content front and center, positioning you as someone worth following for insights

For B2B marketers and founders, Creator Mode is the fastest organic growth lever on LinkedIn.

## How LinkedIn Creator Mode Works

### Enable It

Go to your LinkedIn profile, click "Creator mode" in the Resources section, and toggle it on. Add up to 5 topic hashtags that describe what you post about. These appear on your profile.

### Content Prioritization

Once enabled, your Activity section is replaced by a Featured section at the top. Your posts, articles, and newsletters become the first thing visitors see instead of your work history.

### Access Creator Tools

Unlock LinkedIn Live for real-time video broadcasting, Audio Events for podcast-style conversations, and Newsletters for recurring long-form content. These tools are only available to profiles with Creator Mode active.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is LinkedIn Creator Mode free?

Yes. It's a free profile setting available to all LinkedIn members. No Premium subscription required.

### Does Creator Mode hurt connection requests?

The "Follow" button replaces "Connect" as the default, but people can still send connection requests from the "More" dropdown. You may get fewer connection requests but more followers. Which is generally better for content reach.

### Should every professional turn on Creator Mode?

Only if you plan to post regularly. Creator Mode benefits active publishers. If you rarely post, the profile layout changes offer little value and may make your profile look empty.
