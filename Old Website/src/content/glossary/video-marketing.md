## What is Video Marketing?

Video marketing is the practice of using video content to promote a brand, product, or service across digital channels. From YouTube and TikTok to email and landing pages.

It's not just about filming something and posting it. Real video marketing ties every piece of footage to a specific goal: awareness, engagement, leads, or sales. Marketers use it alongside [content marketing](/glossary/content-marketing) and [social media marketing](/glossary/social-media-marketing) to reach audiences who'd rather watch than read.

Here's a number worth remembering: 91% of businesses used video as a marketing tool in 2024, according to Wyzowl's annual survey. That's not a trend. It's table stakes.

## Why Does Video Marketing Matter?

Ignoring video means ignoring the format your audience actively prefers. Getting it right changes the math on attention, trust, and revenue.

- **Higher engagement**. Video posts on social media generate 1,200% more shares than text and image posts combined, per Brightcove data
- **Better conversion rates**. Adding video to a landing page can increase conversions by up to 86%, according to EyeView research
- **Stronger [organic reach](/glossary/organic-reach)**. Every major social algorithm prioritizes video content, especially [short-form video](/glossary/short-form-video)
- **Improved retention**. Viewers retain 95% of a message when they watch it in video versus 10% when reading text

If you're a local business or SMB competing for attention against bigger brands, video gives you a disproportionate edge. People trust faces. They trust demos. A 60-second walkthrough of your service builds more credibility than 2,000 words of copy.

## How Video Marketing Works

The process has 3 stages, and skipping any of them is where most businesses stumble.

### Strategy and Planning

Start with one question: what do you want the viewer to do after watching? Subscribe, book a call, visit your site, share with a friend. The answer shapes everything else. Pick your target audience, choose the right platform, and decide on format before you ever hit record.

### Production

You don't need a film crew. A smartphone, decent lighting, and a quiet room handle 80% of use cases. What matters more than production quality is clarity. Say one thing per video. Say it well. Keep it short unless the format demands length (tutorials, webinars).

### Distribution and Optimization

Posting a video once on one platform is a waste. Repurpose it. A 10-minute YouTube video becomes 4 short clips for Instagram Reels, a LinkedIn post, and an embedded asset on your blog. Tag it with the right keywords, write a strong thumbnail, and track [engagement rate](/glossary/engagement-rate) to learn what actually resonates.

## Types of Video Marketing

Video marketing breaks down into 6 main categories:

- **Explainer videos**. Short clips that break down how a product or service works. Common on homepages and landing pages.
- **Testimonial videos**. Real customers sharing their experience. The single most trusted format for building social proof.
- **Product demos**. Walkthroughs showing features in action. Especially effective for SaaS and service businesses.
- **Short-form social videos**. Under 60 seconds, designed for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Highest organic reach potential.
- **Educational/how-to videos**. Tutorial content that answers specific questions. Great for [SEO](/glossary/seo) on YouTube.
- **Live video**. Real-time broadcasts on platforms like Instagram Live or LinkedIn Live. Raw, unedited, high engagement.

The right mix depends on your audience and goals. B2B companies lean on demos and educational content. Local businesses get the most from testimonials and short-form clips.

## Video Marketing Examples

**Example 1: A dental practice using patient testimonials**
A family dentist in Austin records 30-second testimonial clips with happy patients after appointments. They post one per week to Instagram Reels and embed the best ones on their Google Business Profile landing page. Result: 3x more appointment requests from their website within 4 months.

**Example 2: A SaaS company running YouTube tutorials**
A project management tool creates "how to" videos answering the exact questions their target audience searches on YouTube ,  "how to run a sprint planning meeting," "how to track team capacity." Each video links to a free trial. The channel becomes their second-biggest acquisition source behind organic search.

**Example 3: A plumber who doesn't use video at all**
Compare that to a competing plumbing company that posts only text updates on Facebook once a month. Zero video. Their competitor films 15-second "before and after" clips of jobs and posts them to TikTok and Instagram. Guess which one gets calls from homeowners under 40.

## Video Marketing vs. Content Marketing

People confuse these two because video IS content. But the terms describe different scopes.

| | Video Marketing | [Content Marketing](/glossary/content-marketing) |
|---|---|---|
| **Definition** | Marketing through video specifically | Marketing through any content format |
| **Formats** | Reels, YouTube, live, webinars | Blog posts, video, podcasts, infographics, email |
| **Primary goal** | Engagement, awareness, demos | Traffic, leads, authority building |
| **Distribution** | Social platforms, YouTube, websites | Search engines, email, social, syndication |
| **Example** | A 30-second Instagram Reel | A 2,000-word SEO blog post |

Video marketing is a subset of content marketing. You need both. But video tends to drive awareness and engagement faster, while written content drives long-term [organic traffic](/glossary/organic-traffic).

## Video Marketing Best Practices

- **Lead with a hook in the first 3 seconds**. Viewers decide instantly whether to keep watching. Start with the payoff, not an intro.
- **Optimize for silent viewing** ,  85% of Facebook videos are watched without sound. Add captions to every video. Always.
- **Repurpose aggressively**. One long video should produce 4-6 shorter pieces. A YouTube tutorial becomes Reels, Stories, and a blog embed. Services like theStacc handle [content repurposing](/glossary/content-repurposing) and written content automatically, freeing your time for video production.
- **Track the right metrics**. Views are vanity. Watch time, click-through rate, and conversions tell you what's actually working.
- **Post consistently**. One great video per month loses to one decent video per week. Algorithms reward frequency and recency.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does video marketing cost?

A single professional video can cost $1,000-$10,000+. But smartphone-shot content costs almost nothing and often outperforms polished productions on social media. Start with what you have.

### What's the best platform for video marketing?

YouTube for long-form and search traffic. TikTok and Instagram Reels for discovery and [organic reach](/glossary/organic-reach). LinkedIn for B2B audiences. The best platform is wherever your customers already spend time.

### Does video help SEO?

Directly, yes. YouTube is the second-largest search engine. Embedded video increases time on page, which signals quality to Google. And video results appear in standard Google SERPs for many queries.

### How long should marketing videos be?

Social media: 15-60 seconds. YouTube: 7-15 minutes for tutorials. Sales pages: under 2 minutes. Match the format to the platform and the viewer's intent.
