## What is Middle of Funnel (MOFU)?

Middle of funnel is the consideration stage where prospects know they have a problem, understand that solutions exist, and are actively evaluating their options.

MOFU leads are past the "what is this?" phase ([top of funnel](/glossary/top-of-funnel)) but not yet at the "I'm ready to buy" phase ([bottom of funnel](/glossary/bottom-of-funnel)). They're comparing approaches, reading case studies, watching demos, and building a shortlist. The content that wins at this stage isn't educational. It's evaluative. It helps people decide.

According to Gartner, B2B buyers spend 27% of their buying journey researching independently online. Most of that research happens at MOFU. If your brand isn't part of the consideration set during this stage, you won't be part of the final decision.

## Why Does MOFU Matter?

This is where you convert anonymous traffic into identified, engaged prospects. And where most funnels leak.

- **Qualifies interest**. MOFU content separates casual browsers from genuine prospects. Someone who downloads a comparison guide is more serious than someone who read a blog post.
- **Builds preference**. The companies that provide the best MOFU content earn a spot on the shortlist. This is where you differentiate from competitors.
- **Captures leads**. MOFU is where [lead magnets](/glossary/lead-magnet), gated content, and webinar registrations convert visitors into identifiable [leads](/glossary/lead)
- **Shortens sales cycles**. Prospects who consume MOFU content arrive at sales conversations more educated, which means reps spend less time explaining and more time closing

MOFU is the most neglected stage. Companies over-invest in awareness and under-invest in consideration.

## How MOFU Works

### Create Comparison Content

Build pages that compare your solution to alternatives, competitive comparisons, and "best [category] tools" roundups. Prospects are actively searching for these terms. If you don't create this content, your competitors will.

### Offer Gated Resources

Case studies, industry reports, ROI calculators, and templates are perfect MOFU [lead magnets](/glossary/lead-magnet). The exchange (content for contact info) is reasonable at this stage because the prospect already has intent.

### Nurture With Email

Once you've captured a MOFU lead, use [drip campaigns](/glossary/drip-campaign) to continue the conversation. Send relevant case studies, answer common objections, and eventually present a clear path to purchase or demo.

## MOFU Examples

**Example 1: Comparison page strategy**
A SaaS company created "vs." pages comparing their product against each major competitor. These pages ranked for "[competitor] alternative" keywords and became their highest-converting organic content ,  8% visitor-to-lead conversion rate vs. 2% for blog posts.

**Example 2: Webinar funnel**
A marketing agency hosted monthly webinars on topics their prospects cared about. Webinar registrants who also downloaded the post-webinar resource converted to sales meetings at 25%. The webinar served as both content and qualification. Attendees self-identified as interested and engaged.
## Frequently Asked Questions

### What content works best for MOFU?

Case studies, comparison guides, webinars, product demos, and ROI calculators. Anything that helps prospects evaluate options and build confidence in their decision.

### How do you move leads from TOFU to MOFU?

Offer a relevant [lead magnet](/glossary/lead-magnet) on your TOFU content, then use [lead nurturing](/glossary/lead-nurturing) emails to introduce MOFU content. Case studies, comparison guides, and customer stories. The content naturally pulls engaged readers deeper into the funnel.

### What metrics track MOFU performance?

Lead conversion rate (visitors to leads), [marketing qualified leads](/glossary/marketing-qualified-lead) generated, email engagement rates, and content-to-demo or content-to-trial conversion rates. These metrics show whether your MOFU content is actually moving people toward a purchase.
