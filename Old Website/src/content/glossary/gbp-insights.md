## What is GBP Insights?

GBP Insights. Rebranded as "Performance" in Google's updated Business Profile interface. Is the analytics dashboard showing exactly how people discover and interact with your [Google Business Profile](/glossary/google-business-profile) listing.

The data covers three key areas: how people find you (search queries and impression counts), where they find you (Google Search vs Google Maps), and what they do (website clicks, direction requests, phone calls, [message](/glossary/gbp-messaging) interactions). This data is accessible directly in Google Search when you manage your profile or through the GBP dashboard.

For local businesses, this is the closest thing to Google Analytics for your Maps listing. It reveals which keywords trigger your listing, how many people take action after seeing it, and how your visibility trends over time. Google provides up to 6 months of historical data.

## Why Does GBP Insights Matter?

You can't optimize what you don't measure. Insights tells you exactly what's working in your [local SEO](/glossary/local-seo).

- **Reveals your actual search queries**. See the exact terms that trigger your listing, which may differ from your assumptions
- **Tracks customer actions**. Calls, direction requests, and website clicks show conversion volume from local search
- **Measures content impact**. After publishing GBP posts or adding photos, track whether engagement increases
- **Identifies seasonal patterns**. Understand when customers search for your services most and plan content accordingly

Every local business should review their GBP Insights monthly to understand their local search performance.

## How GBP Insights Works

### Accessing the Data

Search for your business name in Google while logged into your business account. Click "Performance" in the profile management panel. You'll see data broken into searches, visitors, direction requests, calls, messages, and bookings. Date range is adjustable up to 6 months.

### Key Metrics

Total searches shows how often your listing appeared. Clicks on your listing shows how many people engaged. Direction requests and calls are your conversion metrics. The query report reveals actual search terms. Goldmine data for understanding what customers type when looking for businesses like yours.

### Acting on the Data

If certain services generate the most search impressions but few clicks, your listing may need better [photos](/glossary/gbp-photos) or [services descriptions](/glossary/gbp-services). If direction requests are high but calls are low, your listing might lack a prominent phone number. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### How far back does GBP Insights data go?

The current Performance dashboard shows up to 6 months of historical data. You can't access data older than that through the interface. Export reports regularly if you want to track year-over-year trends.

### Are GBP Insights accurate?

GBP Insights shows impression and engagement data directly from Google's systems, so it's reliable for trends and relative comparisons. However, absolute numbers should be treated as estimates. Google may sample data for high-volume listings.

### What's the most important metric in GBP Insights?

For most local businesses, direction requests plus calls equals your conversion volume. These are the actions that turn searchers into customers. Total impressions matter for understanding visibility, but action metrics matter for measuring business impact.
