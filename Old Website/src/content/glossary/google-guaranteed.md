## What is Google Guaranteed?

Google Guaranteed is a trust badge displayed on [Local Services Ads (LSAs)](/glossary/local-services-ads-lsas) for home service businesses. Plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, locksmiths, and similar trades. Indicating that Google has verified the business and backs their work with a satisfaction guarantee.

The badge appears as a green checkmark with "Google Guaranteed" next to the business name in LSA results. If a customer books through an LSA and is unsatisfied with the quality of work, Google may reimburse them up to $2,000 (lifetime cap per customer).

This badge is a significant competitive advantage. A Google study found that businesses with the Google Guaranteed badge receive 12% more clicks than those without it. The badge communicates trust in a way that organic rankings and standard ads cannot.

## Why Does Google Guaranteed Matter?

The badge changes consumer behavior at the moment of decision.

- **Instant trust**. In industries plagued by scam concerns (locksmiths, home repair), the Google Guaranteed badge provides third-party validation that no amount of marketing copy can replicate
- **Higher click-through rates**. The green checkmark and "Google Guaranteed" text stand out visually in search results, attracting more clicks
- **Competitive moat**. Not all businesses qualify or bother to apply. Having the badge when competitors don't is an immediate differentiation
- **Customer protection**. The reimbursement guarantee reduces purchase anxiety for customers choosing between verified and unverified service providers

For eligible service businesses, Google Guaranteed is one of the strongest trust signals available online.

## How Google Guaranteed Works

### Qualification Requirements

Businesses must pass Google's screening: background checks on the business owner and field workers, license verification for their trade and state, insurance verification (general liability), and a positive [review](/glossary/review-management) profile. Requirements vary by industry and state.

### Maintaining the Badge

The badge isn't permanent. Google periodically re-verifies license and insurance status. High dispute rates can lead to badge removal. Maintaining a strong [star rating](/glossary/star-rating) and low customer complaint rate is essential for keeping the badge active.

### The Reimbursement Process

If a customer is dissatisfied, they can file a claim with Google. Google reviews the claim and may reimburse up to $2,000 for services booked directly through the LSA. The reimbursement comes from Google, not the business. But excessive claims can result in badge removal and LSA suspension.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get the Google Guaranteed badge?

Apply through the [Local Services Ads](/glossary/local-services-ads-lsas) portal. You'll need to submit business licenses, insurance certificates, and consent to background checks on yourself and your employees. Processing takes 2-5 weeks depending on verification complexity. Google partners with third-party background check services for the screening.

### Does Google Guaranteed cost anything?

The badge itself is free. The cost is running Local Services Ads, which charge per lead. You need an active LSA campaign to display the badge. There's no separate fee for the verification or the guarantee program itself.

### What's the difference between Google Guaranteed and Google Screened?

Google Guaranteed is for home service trades (plumbers, electricians, cleaners) and includes the financial reimbursement guarantee. [Google Screened](/glossary/google-screened) is for professional services (lawyers, accountants, financial advisors) and verifies credentials but doesn't include financial reimbursement. Both require background checks.
