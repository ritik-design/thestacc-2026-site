## What Is Outrank?

Outrank.so is a fully automated SEO content platform. It handles keyword research, article writing, image generation, and CMS publishing without manual input after the initial setup. Founded by Tibo (the creator behind Tweet Hunter and Taplio) and Eugene Zolotarenko, it launched in early 2025 and has grown quickly in the indie SaaS space.

The platform claims 750,000+ articles created, 25,000+ backlinks placed, and 10,000+ ChatGPT mentions secured for users. Its domain rating sits at 72.

Unlike content optimization tools like [Surfer SEO](/reviews/surfer-seo) or [Frase.io](/reviews/frase) that score your writing and expect you to do the work, Outrank handles production end to end. You connect a CMS, pick keywords, and articles publish on schedule. It is closer to an automated content agency than a writing assistant.

What Outrank is *not*: a Local SEO platform, a Google Business Profile management tool, or a technical SEO auditor. It does one thing. Blog content automation. And bets everything on doing that well.

## Outrank Features (2026)

### AI Article Generation

The core feature. Outrank analyzes top-ranking pages for your target keyword, identifies the content structure those pages share, and writes a long-form article (1,200 to 3,000+ words) aligned to that SERP data.

Each article includes a title, meta description, header structure, body content, and a featured image. The system publishes one article per day to your CMS automatically.

Content quality sits above generic AI tools but below professional human writers. Users consistently report that 70 to 80 percent of articles are publishable without editing. The remaining 20 to 30 percent need a light pass for accuracy or tone.

### Competitor Content Analysis

Before writing each article, Outrank scrapes the top-ranking pages for that keyword. This drives the content structure: what headers to include, what subtopics to cover, what length to target. The result is output shaped by what Google is already rewarding.

This is Outrank's strongest technical feature. Content built from live SERP analysis outperforms content built from prompts alone because it reflects actual ranking signals.

### Automated Keyword Research

Built-in keyword research surfaces volume, difficulty, and intent data. The system generates a content calendar from your targets, clustering related topics into a publishing sequence for [topical authority](/glossary/topical-authority) building over time.

For business owners who do not want to manage keyword strategy manually, this saves 5 to 10 hours per month compared to doing research in [Ahrefs](/reviews/ahrefs) or [Semrush](/reviews/semrush) and then briefing writers.

### Multi-Platform CMS Publishing

Outrank supports direct publishing to:

- WordPress
- Webflow
- Shopify
- Notion
- Wix
- Framer
- Ghost
- Custom Webhook (any CMS)

This is the broadest CMS support in the automated content category. Most competitors cover WordPress and Webflow only. For teams on Shopify, Wix, or Framer, this integration breadth is a genuine advantage.

### Content Scheduling and Calendar

A visual content calendar lets you set publishing frequency and manage the article queue. Publishing modes include draft-only, requires-approval, and full auto-publish. The "while you sleep" experience is real for users who configure this correctly.

### The Backlink Exchange Network. What You Need to Know

This is the feature no other Outrank review covers plainly. Outrank's built-in backlink exchange automatically places links from your published articles to other Outrank users' sites, and vice versa, based on niche relevance matching.

**How it works:** When you publish articles through Outrank, the system embeds outbound links to other users' sites within your content. In return, other users' articles link back to yours. The exchange is automated. You do not approve individual link placements.

**Why this matters:** Google's [link spam policies](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/essentials/spam-policies#link-spam) state that "any links that are intended to manipulate PageRank or a site's ranking may be considered link spam." The policy specifically flags "link exchange schemes."

Automated link exchanges, where links are placed programmatically across multiple sites without editorial intent, sit in territory these policies directly address. Google has historically taken action against private blog networks and link exchange schemes when the pattern becomes identifiable at scale.

**The fair assessment:** Outrank uses niche relevance matching and contextual body placement, which are better signals than footer links or irrelevant exchanges. Whether Google identifies the network pattern at scale is the open variable. What is clear: this is a practice Google's guidelines explicitly discourage.

Users report domain rating improvements: DR 13 to 36 in 4 months (Moonb), DR 5 to 25 in under 2 months (MassBlogger). But isolating the backlink exchange impact from content publishing impact alone is difficult.

> **Insight:** Most users who report positive results from the backlink exchange are niche site builders and affiliate marketers, where short-term ranking velocity is worth accepting more risk. Local service businesses that depend on their domain's long-term reputation should evaluate this feature with specific care. You can use Outrank with the backlink exchange disabled. The content automation works independently of the link exchange.

### Free Tools Suite and Tools Builder

Outrank includes a suite of free SEO tools (title generator, blog outline generator, [schema markup](/glossary/structured-data) creator, keyword density analyzer, and more) plus a "tools builder" that lets you create SEO-optimized free tools on your own site.

This is a smart programmatic SEO play. Free tools attract [backlinks](/glossary/backlinks) naturally and build [topical authority](/glossary/topical-authority) without relying on the exchange network.

### Google Search Console Integration

Connect your Google Search Console account to track rankings and organic traffic for published content. Outrank uses this data to surface gaps. Keywords you appear for but do not rank well. And can inform content strategy adjustments over time.

### Additional Features

- **Domain authority tracking** to monitor DR/DA over time
- **AI image generation** for featured and body images
- **Multi-language support** for content in 150+ languages
- **Unlimited AI rewrites** for editing published articles
- **Multi-site dashboard** for agencies managing multiple domains

## How to Use Outrank: The 6-Step Workflow

Most new users expect rankings in 30 days. That expectation leads to cancellations before the tool has had time to work. Here is how experienced users set up the system for results.

**Step 1: Audit your domain before connecting.** Check your existing domain authority and published content count. Content velocity on a low-authority domain takes longer to convert into rankings. Set realistic expectations based on where you are starting.

**Step 2: Do keyword research before handing off.** Outrank's built-in keyword tool is functional. Supplement it with a pass in [Ahrefs](/reviews/ahrefs) or Google Keyword Planner. Identify your 20 to 30 highest-priority keywords by volume and commercial intent. Input quality directly affects output quality.

**Step 3: Start on "Requires Approval" publishing.** Read the first 10 articles before setting full auto-publish. Calibrate quality expectations and catch systematic issues (tone, accuracy, brand fit) before they go live at scale.

**Step 4: Evaluate the backlink exchange deliberately.** The exchange is opt-in. Before enabling it, decide whether the risk profile fits your domain and goals. A niche affiliate site has different risk tolerance than a medical practice domain built over 10 years.

**Step 5: Connect Google Search Console from day one.** Capture impressions data from your first article. Set a calendar reminder for 60 to 90 days. Early GSC signals (impressions appearing before ranking) confirm correct indexing.

**Step 6: Review and refresh at 6 months.** Run a content audit. Which articles get impressions but not clicks? Which sit on page 2 ready for a refresh? The system generates new content well. The gap is ongoing optimization of what is already published.

## Outrank FAQ

**What is Outrank?**

Outrank (outrank.so) is an automated SEO content platform that handles keyword research, article writing, image generation, and CMS publishing without manual involvement after setup. It positions itself as a "blog on autopilot" tool for business owners, agencies, and niche site builders who want organic content published consistently without hiring writers or managing a workflow.

**How much does Outrank cost?**

Outrank's Standard plan costs $99 per month for 30 articles. Upgrade tiers run $149 per month (60 articles) and $199 per month (90 articles). A $1 three-day trial is available with full access. Enterprise pricing is custom for agencies.

**Is there a free trial for Outrank?**

Outrank offers a $1 three-day trial with full access. Articles published during the trial include an Outrank watermark. No free tier with full article generation and auto-publishing exists.

**Does Outrank write content for you?**

Yes. Outrank generates full-length SEO articles (1,200 to 3,000+ words) from your target keywords, including title, meta description, headers, and body content. Articles are built from SERP analysis. The system analyzes top-ranking competitor pages before generating each article. Unlike Surfer SEO or Frase, you do not write anything; the system handles production end to end.

**What is Outrank's backlink exchange, and is it safe?**

Outrank's backlink exchange automatically places contextual links from your published articles to other Outrank users' sites in relevant niches, and vice versa. The intent is passive link building alongside content publishing. Google's [link spam policies](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/essentials/spam-policies#link-spam) flag automated link exchange schemes. This is a real risk factor to evaluate before enabling. You can use Outrank with the backlink exchange disabled; the content automation works independently.

**Does Outrank do local SEO?**

No. Outrank is focused entirely on website blog SEO. It has no Google Business Profile management, local citation building, or Google Maps ranking features. Local service businesses that need Google Maps traffic need a separate local SEO tool. Which adds $29 to $79 per month to the real total cost of using Outrank.
