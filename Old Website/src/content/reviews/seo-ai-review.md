## What Is SEO.AI?

SEO.AI is an SEO autopilot platform built to automate the full content pipeline. Keyword research, content gap analysis, article writing, internal linking, image generation, and direct CMS publishing. All without a human writer.

The company was founded in 2022 by Torbjorn Flensted and Daniel Hojris Baek. Headquarters are in Kongens Lyngby, Denmark. The founding team brings 20+ years of SEO agency experience. The team is small (11-50 employees per LinkedIn), independently funded with no disclosed external investment.

The core problem SEO.AI solves: businesses need SEO content at volume but cannot justify a $1,000-$5,000/month agency retainer. SEO.AI automates the production and publishing so business owners never touch a Google Doc.

![What SEO.AI does: the full content automation pipeline](/images/blog/seo-ai-review-pipeline.webp)

Here is what most SEO.AI reviews get wrong. The platform runs **two completely different products** for two different buyers:

- **Self-Serve Tool Plans** ($149-$299/month): You log in, use the AI tools, and produce articles with manual direction. The tools assist. You operate.
- **Teammate Autopilot Plans** (custom pricing): The system runs the entire monthly SEO workflow autonomously. Gap analysis, planning, writing, linking, publishing. Zero ongoing input from you.

Buying the wrong tier is the single most common source of disappointment. The self-serve plan is a toolkit. The Teammate plan is a service. They solve different problems at different price points.

## SEO.AI Features Breakdown

### Content Gap Analysis

SEO.AI scans your site against competitors and identifies keyword gaps. Topics your competitors rank for that you do not cover. The output is a prioritized article list with keyword targets and topic clustering already applied.

This is genuinely the hardest part of the [SEO content workflow](/blog/seo-content-writing) for small business owners. Most do not know what to write because they have no competitive visibility. SEO.AI turns the analysis directly into a content plan with zero spreadsheet work.

### AI Article Writing

The platform writes long-form SEO articles (2,000-5,000+ words) structured around target keywords and SERP research. Internal links are inserted automatically. Featured images are generated per article using AI.

Brand Voice configuration lets you upload 2-3 examples of your preferred tone. The AI calibrates to your style instead of generic template output. Users consistently cite this as the feature that separates SEO.AI from basic AI writers.

Content quality is rated as publish-ready for businesses currently producing zero content. Users who want varied formatting (pull quotes, custom bullet styles, visual variety) report needing light editing before publishing.

Publishing modes include Auto Publish, Requires Approval (staged as drafts), and No Auto Publish. The approval step gives businesses automation with a human quality gate.

### Multi-Language Support

50+ languages with full end-to-end automation in each. Each language counts as a separate site slot. An international e-commerce brand running content in English, German, and French needs the Multi Site plan ($299/month).

Compare that to agency costs of $5,000-$15,000/month for equivalent multilingual output. This is SEO.AI's most defensible differentiator.

The details are limited in public documentation, but the concept addresses a gap that pure content tools ignore entirely: [link building](/blog/link-building-strategies) alongside content publishing.

### SEO Analytics

Google Search Console integration tracks rankings, impressions, and clicks directly inside the platform. No third-party rank tracker needed. Useful for monitoring which content earns visibility over time.

## How to Use SEO.AI: Setup Workflow

The most common mistake: signing up for the self-serve tier when you want Teammate autopilot, or vice versa. Here is the 6-step setup.

**Step 1: Choose your tier.** Teammate Autopilot = hands-off. Self-Serve = manual control. Not interchangeable.

**Step 2: Connect your CMS.** WordPress requires an Application Password (not your login password). Webflow and Shopify connect via OAuth. Users report connection completing in under 5 minutes.

**Step 3: Complete your business profile.** Define business type, target area, primary keywords, and competitor URLs. The gap analysis accuracy depends on the context you provide.

**Step 4: Configure Brand Voice.** Upload 2-3 content examples. The AI trains on your tone. Optional but consistently improves output.

**Step 5: Review the content plan.** SEO.AI generates the first content calendar. Review planned topics. Remove misaligned articles. Approve the queue.

**Step 6: Set publishing mode and monitor quarterly.** Start with Requires Approval for 30 days to validate quality. Switch to Auto Publish once satisfied. Check Google Search Console data quarterly. [Ranking movement](/blog/seo-statistics) typically begins at 60-90 days.
