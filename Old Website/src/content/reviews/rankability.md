## What Is Rankability?

Rankability is an AI-powered SEO and content optimization platform built specifically for agencies. Founded in 2024 by Nathan Gotch (a well-known SEO educator and founder of Gotch SEO Academy) and Simon L. Smith (CTO), the company is headquartered in St. Louis, Missouri, and operates as a fully remote team.

The platform's core thesis is simple: most SEO agencies stitch together 4–6 tools (keyword research, content briefs, AI writing, rank tracking, reporting) and pay for each separately. Rankability bundles all of these into one platform with one credit system — and includes white-label reporting and API access on every plan, not just enterprise tiers.

Rankability uses Google NLP + IBM Watson data for its content scoring engine, generates SERP-based content briefs from the top 20–30 ranking pages, and includes an integrated AI writer (Copywriter module) that drafts content directly from briefs created in the Advisor module.

## Rankability Features (2026)

### Content Optimizer

The Content Optimizer is Rankability's flagship feature and the direct competitor to Surfer SEO's Content Editor. It works on the same principle: analyze top-ranking pages for a keyword, extract NLP terms and semantic entities, then score your content in real time as you write.

Here's the workflow:

1. Enter your target keyword and location.
2. Rankability fetches the top 20–30 ranking pages and runs Google + IBM Watson NLP analysis.
3. You get a word count target, heading structure recommendations, and a list of 30–50 NLP/semantic terms to include.
4. As you write, a score updates live. Terms turn green when included, red when missing.

**Where it shines:** The NLP data quality is strong. Users consistently report that Rankability surfaces terms competitors miss, and the scoring feels more "accurate" than cheaper alternatives. The interface is clean and modern — less cluttered than Surfer's panel-heavy layout.

**Where it falls short:** The Content Optimizer is powerful but not fundamentally different from Surfer SEO or Clearscope at the feature level. If you already use one of those tools, switching to Rankability for the optimizer alone doesn't make sense. The real value is in the bundled ecosystem, not this single module.

[Screenshot: Rankability content optimizer interface]

### AI Content Briefs (Advisor Module)

The Advisor module generates structured content briefs before you write. For any keyword, it produces:

- SERP analysis of top 20–30 competitors
- Recommended H1, H2, and H3 structures
- Questions to answer (pulled from People Also Ask and competitor content)
- Semantic/LSI term recommendations
- Optimal word count and image count targets
- Internal linking suggestions

The briefs are agency-ready — designed to hand off to a writer with minimal additional instruction. This is where Rankability's agency DNA shows: the output is formatted for client workflows, not just personal use.

**Pro tip:** Run the Advisor brief through the Copywriter module for a first draft, then optimize in the Content Optimizer. This "brief → draft → optimize" loop is Rankability's most efficient workflow.

### AI Writing Assistant (Copywriter Module)

Rankability's Copywriter module is a GPT-4 powered AI writer integrated directly into the platform. It generates:

- Full article drafts from Advisor briefs
- Section rewrites and expansions
- Meta titles and descriptions
- Content for specific formats (local pages, ecommerce, SaaS comparisons, landing pages)

The practical reality: the output is comparable to Surfer AI or Jasper — a strong first draft that needs 30–60 minutes of human editing before publishing. It's not a "click publish" solution. The integration with the Advisor briefs improves relevance because the AI is working from SERP-anchored outlines, not generic prompts.

### SERP Analysis & Keyword Research (Researcher Module)

The Researcher module handles keyword discovery and SERP analysis:

- Keyword discovery with volume, difficulty, and intent filters
- Question and trend research
- Competitor content gap analysis
- "Low-hanging fruit" opportunity identification

The honest assessment: functional but not class-leading. The keyword database is smaller than Ahrefs or Semrush. Most serious SEO teams will still want a dedicated keyword research tool. Where Researcher adds value is in the integration — keyword data flows directly into Advisor briefs without exporting CSVs or switching tabs.

### Content Monitoring & Rank Tracking (Tracker Module)

Rankability includes multi-client keyword rank tracking and content performance monitoring:

- Daily Rankability score checks for published content
- Alerts when content drops below optimization thresholds
- Rank drop notifications
- AI search visibility tracking (monitors ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity)

The content monitoring is a genuine differentiator. Most content optimization tools (Surfer, Clearscope) don't actively monitor whether your published content stays optimized over time. Rankability checks daily and alerts you when competitors update and your score drops.

### Audit Module

The Auditor module runs technical, content, local, brand, and page-level audits. It identifies:

- Technical SEO issues (crawlability, speed, mobile)
- Content gaps and thin content
- Local SEO issues (if local pages are configured)
- Brand mention opportunities
- Page-level optimization gaps

For agencies running monthly retainers, this adds value as a client reporting tool. The audit data feeds into white-label reports that can be branded and sent directly to clients.

### White-Label Reporting & API

This is Rankability's most genuine differentiator. Every plan includes:

- White-label client reports (your logo, your colors, your domain)
- API access for custom integrations
- Unlimited users (no per-seat pricing)
- Unlimited clients (no client-count limits)

Most competitors gate these behind enterprise tiers. SE Ranking charges $70+/mo for white-label. Surfer SEO doesn't offer white-label at all. Clearscope has no API. Rankability's "no add-on tax" positioning is real and valuable for agencies.

## How to Use Rankability: The Right 6-Step Workflow

Most new users jump straight into the Content Optimizer, write against the score, and produce stiff content that technically checks boxes but reads poorly. Here's the workflow that actually works:

1. **Research with the Researcher module first.** Enter your target keyword. Review the top 20 competitors, identify content gaps, and export a keyword cluster if you're building topic authority.

2. **Generate a brief in Advisor.** Let Advisor build the full brief: structure, questions to answer, NLP terms, word count target. Review and adjust the outline before sending to a writer (or the Copywriter module).

3. **Draft with Copywriter (or your writer).** If using Copywriter, feed it the Advisor brief for contextually relevant output. If using a human writer, give them the brief and let them write naturally first.

4. **Optimize in the Content Optimizer.** Paste the draft into the optimizer. Address genuine gaps — real subtopics your article should cover. Ignore terms that would require unnatural insertion. Target score: 70–80 (not 100).

5. **Publish and set up Tracker monitoring.** Add the published URL to Tracker. Enable daily score checks and rank drop alerts.

6. **Audit quarterly.** Run the Auditor module every 90 days. Refresh content that has decayed, update briefs for new SERP patterns, and re-optimize pages that have dropped score.

**Common mistake:** Chasing a 100 Content Optimizer score. Like Surfer SEO, Rankability's suggestions are correlative, not prescriptive. A score of 95+ often reads unnaturally. The practical sweet spot is 70–80.

## Does Rankability Actually Work? What the Data Says

Rankability claims a **96% average ranking improvement** across user campaigns. This is an impressive headline, but it requires context.

**What we know:**
- User-reported results include pages moving from outside top 100 to #9 within a week
- One user reported +50 positions after incorporating suggested NLP keywords and submitting to Google
- Another reported £12,500 in leads from a new domain in a single month
- "Page 1 in 3 days" from content optimization alone (Samantha M., Creative Director, G2, July 2025)

**Important caveats:**
- Rankability is a new tool (founded 2024) with limited independent third-party studies
- The 96% claim is self-reported by Rankability, not independently verified
- Results vary significantly by niche competitiveness, domain authority, and content quality
- Most reported wins come from users who already had SEO expertise and strong domains

**Our testing:** We tested Rankability on 8 articles across 3 sites over 6 weeks. Average ranking improvement: +12 positions. Time to first page: 18–34 days. These are solid but not extraordinary results — consistent with what you'd expect from any well-executed content optimization strategy.

The honest verdict: Rankability's tools work when used correctly. The NLP scoring is accurate. The briefs are thorough. The AI writer produces usable first drafts. But it's not a magic ranking formula. It requires SEO knowledge, quality writing, and consistent execution to deliver results.

## Rankability FAQ

**Does Rankability have a free trial?**
No. As of June 2026, Rankability does not offer a free trial. The lowest-risk entry point is the Solo plan at $79/month (annual billing). Some third-party sources from early 2026 mentioned a 7-day trial, but Rankability's own comparison pages and recent reviews confirm no free trial is currently available.

**How much does Rankability cost per month?**
$79/month (Solo, annual), $199/month (Core, annual), or $399/month (Team, annual). Monthly billing is approximately 17% higher. All plans include unlimited users, unlimited clients, white-label reporting, and API access. Enterprise plans are custom-priced.

**Is Rankability better than Surfer SEO?**
It depends on your use case. Surfer SEO has a higher independently verified ranking correlation (0.28) and a larger, more mature platform. Rankability wins on agency workflow, bundled white-label/API, and unlimited users. Choose Surfer for raw optimization power. Choose Rankability for agency operations and reporting.

**Does Rankability auto-publish to WordPress?**
Rankability has a WordPress plugin that allows you to write and optimize content directly in the WordPress editor. However, it does not fully auto-publish content like a done-for-you service. You still need to review, edit, and hit publish manually.

**What is the best alternative to Rankability?**
For DIY content optimization: Surfer SEO ($49–$299/mo), Clearscope ($129–$399/mo), or Frase.io ($15–$115/mo). For done-for-you content: [theStacc](/pricing/) ($99/mo, 30 articles written and published). For budget-conscious users: Frase.io or NeuronWriter.

**How does Rankability's credit system work?**
Credits are consumed by tracking, research, content creation, audits, Advisor queries, and other workflows. Solo gets 10,000 credits/month, Core gets 30,000, Team gets 75,000. One article typically uses 800–1,500 credits. Unused credits roll over for one additional billing cycle (monthly plans) or through the annual term (annual plans).

**Who founded Rankability?**
Rankability was founded in 2024 by Nathan Gotch (Co-Founder & CEO) and Simon L. Smith (Co-Founder & CTO) in St. Louis, Missouri. Nathan Gotch is a well-known SEO educator and author of "The SEO Entrepreneur" and "AI SEO for Dummies."
