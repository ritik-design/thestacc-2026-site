## What Is SE Ranking?

SE Ranking is an all-in-one SEO platform founded in 2013 by Valery Kurilov. The company is headquartered in London with additional offices in Palo Alto, Kyiv, Wroclaw, and Warsaw. It is bootstrapped — no venture capital, no outside funding. The company reports hundreds of thousands of users worldwide and has grown consistently without outside investment.

The platform started as a rank tracker and expanded into a full SEO suite covering keyword research, site auditing, backlink analysis, competitor research, content editing, and AI search visibility tracking. The core thesis: most SEO teams do not need every feature Semrush offers. They need reliable rank tracking, solid audits, and actionable keyword data at a price that does not eat their margin.

What SE Ranking is *not*: a content creation service, an automated publishing platform, or a done-for-you SEO system. It tells you where you rank, what is broken on your site, and what keywords your competitors target. You still write every article, optimize every page, and publish every piece of content manually.

The company maintains a 4.8/5 rating on G2 with over 700 reviews. Customer support scores 9.4/10. Users consistently praise the price-to-feature ratio and the quality of rank tracking data. ## SE Ranking Features (2026)

### Keyword Rank Tracker

The rank tracker is SE Ranking's original feature and still its strongest. It monitors keyword positions across Google, Bing, and Yahoo with daily updates. You can track rankings by location down to the city level, by device type (desktop vs. mobile), and by SERP feature type (featured snippets, People Also Ask, local pack, AI Overviews).

Key capabilities:

- Daily position updates across multiple search engines
- Competitor tracking for up to 5 competitors per project
- SERP feature monitoring including AI Overviews and featured snippets
- Visibility Score that aggregates your overall search presence into a single metric
- Automated ranking alerts when positions change significantly
- Historical data from the day you create your account

The accuracy is the standout here. SE Ranking's rank tracking correlates well with independent benchmarks and Google Search Console data. Users report that position data is reliable for most keywords, with minor variance on volatile SERP features like AI Overviews and local packs where rankings shift faster than daily crawls can capture.

> **Insight:** SE Ranking's rank tracker updates daily. For most keywords, this is sufficient. For high-volatility niches where positions change multiple times per day, dedicated rank tracking tools with on-demand refresh may be more responsive. But those tools typically start at $129/month or more. SE Ranking gives you reliable tracking at less than half the cost.

### Website Audit

SE Ranking's site audit crawls up to 250,000 pages per month on the Core plan and 2 million on Growth. It checks over 115 technical SEO parameters across crawlability, indexability, page speed, security, and on-page optimization.

The audit categorizes issues by severity: errors, warnings, and notices. The Health Score quantifies your site's technical condition on a 0–100 scale. What distinguishes SE Ranking from basic audit tools is the progress quantification: it tells you exactly how many points your Health Score will improve by fixing specific issue categories.

Key checks include:

- Broken links and redirect chains
- Duplicate content and canonical errors
- Missing or duplicate meta titles and descriptions
- Core Web Vitals performance metrics
- HTTPS implementation and security headers
- JavaScript rendering for dynamic pages
- Internal linking structure and orphan pages
- Structured data validation

The audit supports scheduled recurring crawls. For agencies managing multiple client sites, this automation eliminates the manual work of remembering to re-audit every month.

### Backlink Checker

SE Ranking's backlink analysis monitors referring domains, anchor text distribution, new and lost links, and domain authority scores. The tool identifies toxic backlinks and helps prepare disavow files for Google Search Console submission.

Key capabilities:

- Backlink profile overview with trend graphs
- New and lost backlink monitoring
- Anchor text distribution analysis
- Referring domain authority scoring
- Toxic link identification
- Competitor backlink comparison

The honest assessment: the backlink index is materially smaller than Ahrefs' 35+ trillion links. For pure link building and digital PR workflows, Ahrefs remains the superior tool. SE Ranking's backlink data refreshes weekly rather than daily, which matters for time-sensitive link building campaigns. For routine backlink monitoring and competitor analysis, it is sufficient. For deep link prospecting, it is not.

At theStacc, we do not rely on backlink indexes for our content strategy. Our articles earn links through quality and relevance. But if link building is a primary channel for your business, theStacc supplements research with manual outreach. SE Ranking gives you the data to identify targets. You still need to craft pitches, build relationships, and secure placements yourself.

### Competitor Research

The competitor research module lets you reverse-engineer any competitor's organic strategy:

- Organic keywords: every keyword a domain ranks for, with position, traffic estimate, and SERP features
- Top pages: which pages drive the most organic traffic
- Keyword gap analysis: keywords your competitors rank for that you do not
- Paid search visibility: competitor ad copy and landing page tracking
- Traffic estimates: approximate organic traffic based on ranking positions and search volumes

The keyword gap tool is particularly useful for content planning. Enter your domain plus 2–3 competitors, and SE Ranking surfaces keywords where they have visibility and you do not. Filter by difficulty and volume to find realistic targets.

The limitation: the keyword database contains approximately 5.4 billion keywords across 188 countries. This sounds large, and it is. But Semrush indexes 26+ billion keywords. Ahrefs covers 28+ billion. For hyper-niche B2B topics and non-English markets, SE Ranking's coverage is thinner. You may find zero volume data for long-tail terms where Semrush shows 50–200 monthly searches.

### Content Editor

SE Ranking's Content Editor generates SEO-optimized content briefs and provides real-time optimization scoring as you write. The AI Writer produces article drafts based on competitor analysis and target keywords.

Key capabilities:

- AI-generated content briefs with structure, keywords, and word count targets
- Real-time content scoring against top-ranking competitors
- Google Docs add-on for writing within your existing workflow
- Tone of voice customization
- AI section summaries for faster editing

The practical reality: the Content Editor is functional but not class-leading. Independent reviews consistently describe the AI-generated output as generic and surface-level, requiring 4+ hours of editing per article. The scoring system often requires manual adjustments. For teams that already have writers, the Content Editor provides useful guidance. For teams expecting publish-ready drafts, it falls short of dedicated AI writing tools.

> **Insight:** SE Ranking's Content Editor improved significantly in 2025–2026, but it is still positioned as a secondary feature. The platform's core strength remains research and tracking, not content creation. If your primary need is writing assistance, tools like Surfer SEO, Clearscope, or Frase deliver better optimization scoring. If you need fully written articles, theStacc or other done-for-you services are the appropriate category.

## How to Use SE Ranking: The Right 5-Step Workflow

Most SE Ranking users do this wrong. They set up rank tracking for 200 keywords, run one site audit, then check the dashboard weekly without producing content. Here is the workflow that actually produces results.

**Step 1: Run the Site Audit first, not keyword research**
Fix what's broken before building new. A site with 50 crawl errors and broken redirects will not rank no matter how many articles you publish. Clean your technical foundation first. This takes 1–2 hours with SE Ranking's audit.

**Step 2: Use Competitor Research to find realistic targets**
Do not chase keywords because they have high volume. Use the Keyword Gap tool to find keywords where your closest competitors (similar domain authority) rank in positions 4–20. These are the gaps you can actually fill.

**Step 3: Build topic clusters, not keyword lists**
Group related keywords into clusters. One pillar page + 5–8 supporting articles per cluster. This builds topical authority faster than scattered one-off posts. SE Ranking's Keyword Grouper clusters by SERP similarity.

**Step 4: Create content briefs and hand them to writers**
For each target keyword, use the Content Editor to generate a brief. It gives you word count targets, semantically related terms, and top-10 competitor analysis. Hand this to your writers. Do not just say "write about this keyword."

**Step 5: Track rankings and audit quarterly**
Set up Position Tracking for every published article. Review quarterly. If a page drops from position 5 to position 15, it needs updating. Not replacing. The Page Changes Monitor on Growth+ plans alerts you when competitors update their content.

**What is SE Ranking?**
SE Ranking is an all-in-one SEO platform founded in 2013. It includes keyword rank tracking, website auditing, backlink analysis, competitor research, keyword research, and content editing tools. Used by over 1 million SEO professionals and businesses worldwide.
