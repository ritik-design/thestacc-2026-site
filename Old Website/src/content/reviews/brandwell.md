## What Is BrandWell?

BrandWell is an AI content generation platform that produces long-form blog articles from a keyword, URL, video, or podcast input. The headline feature. RankWell. Generates 2,000–5,000 word articles using a proprietary model trained to produce "undetectable" AI content that scores well on AI detection tools.

The company started as "Content at Scale" in 2022, founded by Justin McGill. It positioned itself as the alternative to generic AI writers (Jasper, Copy.ai). Focusing specifically on long-form SEO content rather than short-form copywriting. The rebrand to BrandWell in 2024 expanded the positioning to include brand voice, content strategy, and detection-proof writing.

The core technology layers multiple AI models together and applies post-processing to reduce AI detection signals (perplexity and burstiness patterns that detectors flag). The result: articles that score lower on GPTZero and Originality.ai than raw ChatGPT output.

What BrandWell is *not*: a fully automated publishing service. It generates drafts that need human review, editing for accuracy, brand voice alignment, and fact-checking. The WordPress integration exists, but most users edit before publishing.

## BrandWell Features (2026)

### RankWell (AI Article Generator)

The flagship. Input a keyword, and RankWell generates:
- Full 2,000–5,000 word article with H2/H3 structure
- NLP-optimized content based on top-ranking SERP analysis
- Built-in SEO scoring (content grade)
- Automatic internal linking suggestions
- Meta title and description

Output quality is above average for AI writing tools. Articles read more naturally than raw ChatGPT output and include more structured sections, data points, and topical coverage.

> **Insight:** RankWell's articles consistently score 70–85 on content optimization tools (Surfer, NeuronWriter). That's competitive with human writers using optimization tools. At a fraction of the time. The catch: factual accuracy isn't guaranteed. Every article needs a human reviewer to verify claims, statistics, and brand-specific details.

### AI Detection Bypass

BrandWell's marketing emphasizes "undetectable" AI content. The post-processing pipeline adjusts sentence structure, vocabulary variation, and pattern randomization to reduce AI detection scores.

Reality: AI detection tools are getting better. "Undetectable" in 2024 doesn't mean undetectable in 2026. More importantly, Google's position is that AI content is fine if it's helpful. Making detection bypass increasingly irrelevant for SEO purposes.

### Content Audit

Analyzes existing published content and suggests:
- Articles that need updating
- Content gaps in your topic coverage
- Optimization opportunities for existing pages

### WordPress Integration

Direct publishing from BrandWell to WordPress. Articles can be pushed as drafts or published posts. This is more integration than most AI writing tools offer.

### Bulk Generation

Generate multiple articles simultaneously. Upload a CSV of keywords, and BrandWell produces articles for each. Useful for content sprints targeting large keyword clusters.

### The Real Cost of BrandWell

The headline pricing understates the real cost in two ways:

1. **You need more articles than the plan includes.** 30 articles/month requires the Professional plan ($499/month), not the Starter ($249/month).

2. **Every article needs editing.** BrandWell's own guidelines recommend 30–60 minutes of human editing per article. At 30 articles, that's 15–30 hours of editing labor per month. If your time is worth $50/hour (conservative for a business owner), that's $750–$1,500 in labor.

> **$499 + $750–$1,500 = $1,249–$1,999/month**. The real cost to publish 30 articles using BrandWell. Cheaper than hiring writers directly ($2,400–$7,500), but significantly more than theStacc's $99/month for 30 publish-ready articles.

## How to Use BrandWell: The Right 4-Step Workflow

**Step 1: Build your keyword list externally**
BrandWell generates articles from keywords but doesn't research keywords for you. Use Semrush, Ahrefs, or Google Keyword Planner to build your target list first.

**Step 2: Generate articles in batches using bulk upload**
Upload your keyword CSV and let BrandWell generate 10–25 articles simultaneously. Batching is more efficient than one-at-a-time generation.

**Step 3: Edit every article before publishing (30–60 min each)**
Review for factual accuracy, brand voice, and topical gaps. AI generates plausible-sounding content that may contain inaccuracies. Check every statistic, claim, and recommendation.

**Step 4: Publish via WordPress integration or manual upload**
Use the WordPress integration for efficiency. For Webflow, Ghost, or other CMS platforms, you'll need to copy-paste and format manually.

## BrandWell FAQ

**What is BrandWell?**
BrandWell (formerly Content at Scale) is an AI content generation platform that produces long-form blog articles (2,000–5,000 words) from a single keyword input. Uses proprietary RankWell technology with built-in NLP optimization.

**How much does BrandWell cost?**
Starter: $249/month (10 articles). Professional: $499/month (25 articles). Enterprise: $999/month (50+ articles).

**Is BrandWell the same as Content at Scale?**
Yes. Content at Scale rebranded to BrandWell in late 2024. Same product, same team, expanded positioning around "brand-building content."

**Does BrandWell pass AI detection?**
BrandWell applies post-processing to reduce AI detection signals. Results vary. Output scores lower on detectors than raw ChatGPT but isn't guaranteed to pass. More importantly, Google has stated AI content can rank if helpful.

**Is BrandWell worth it in 2026?**
For agencies with editors: yes ,  $20–$25/article is cheaper than freelancers. For businesses wanting hands-off publishing: no. Every article needs 30–60 minutes of editing.

**What are the best BrandWell alternatives?**
For AI writing: Jasper ($59/seat), Koala AI ($49/mo), Junia AI ($15/mo). For done-for-you content: theStacc ($99/mo, 30 articles auto-published with no editing needed).

**Does BrandWell publish to WordPress?**
Yes. Direct WordPress integration for pushing articles as drafts or published posts. No native integration for Webflow, Ghost, or other CMS platforms.

**Does BrandWell do local SEO?**
No. No Google Business Profile, local citations, or Google Maps features.

**How many articles can I generate per month?**
Depends on plan: 10 (Starter), 25 (Professional), 50+ (Enterprise). Additional articles may be available as add-ons.

**Does BrandWell do keyword research?**
No. You need a separate keyword research tool (Semrush, Ahrefs, etc.). BrandWell generates content from keywords you provide.

**How much editing does BrandWell content need?**
BrandWell recommends 30–60 minutes per article for fact-checking, brand voice alignment, and accuracy verification. Output is a strong first draft, not publish-ready content.

**Can BrandWell replace a content team?**
It can replace writers for first draft generation but cannot replace editors, fact-checkers, or publishing coordinators. Content review is still required.
