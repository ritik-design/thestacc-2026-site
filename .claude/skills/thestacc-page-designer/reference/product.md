# What theStacc Is

theStacc is an **AI-powered SEO automation platform** that researches, writes, and publishes content on behalf of customers, plus manages their local search presence and social distribution. Founded by **Siddharth Gangal**.

The product replaces three things buyers currently pay for separately: an SEO content agency, a Google Business Profile manager, and a social media scheduler. theStacc unifies all three into one subscription, run by AI, with zero human-in-the-loop required.

## The product in one sentence

> "AI that writes, ranks, and publishes content across Google, AI search, Maps, and every major social channel — automatically."

## What theStacc is NOT (anti-positioning)

- Not another SEO scoring tool (Semrush/Ahrefs are scorers — theStacc is a publisher)
- Not a generic AI writer (Jasper/Copy.ai produce drafts — theStacc produces ranked pages)
- Not a do-everything platform (no CRM, no email, no analytics dashboard for non-SEO data)
- Not for one-off projects — it's a subscription that compounds over months
