title: Add-ons & Managed Services
---

 <p>Most of theStacc is self-service software: AI does the work, you review and approve. But you have two ways to go further. <strong>Add-ons</strong> stack extra capacity onto a plan you already have. <strong>Managed services</strong> hand the entire job to our team, so you do not have to touch the dashboard at all.</p>
<p>This guide covers both.</p>
<h2 id="add-ons">Add-ons<a aria-label="Copy link to Add-ons" class="heading-anchor" href="#add-ons">#</a></h2>
<p>Add-ons are optional extras you can attach to a module subscription. They are billed monthly, on top of your base plan, and you can stack more than one. Add-ons are added to your cart at checkout, right next to your base plan - so you pay for everything in one place, on one card.</p>
<p>There is no separate checkout for an add-on by itself. An add-on always rides along with a base plan for the same module.</p>

<p>If 30 articles a month is not enough, these add more:</p>
<ul><li><strong>Growth Pack - +$50/month</strong> - adds 20 more articles per month, for 50 total.</li><li><strong>Scale Pack - +$100/month</strong> - adds 50 more articles per month, for 80 total.</li></ul>
<p>The extra articles go through the same keyword research, planning, SEO scoring, and publishing flow as your base articles. Nothing else about your setup changes.</p>

<h2 id="managed-services">Managed services<a aria-label="Copy link to Managed services" class="heading-anchor" href="#managed-services">#</a></h2>
<p>Managed services are the opposite of self-service. Instead of you reviewing and approving content, <strong>our team runs everything for you</strong>. We hold the logins, do the work every day, and report back. AI does the heavy lifting on content and posts; a dedicated human manager handles quality checks, strategy, and your monthly check-in call.</p>
<p>This is the right fit if you would rather not log in at all - you just want results, a report, and someone to talk to once a month.</p>
<p>Managed services are not a self-serve checkout. Every tier starts with a conversation, because we need access to your accounts and we tailor the work to your business. Use the <strong>Talk to Our Team</strong> button on the pricing page, or email <strong><a class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="93fbf6fffffcd3e7fbf6e0e7f2f0f0bdf0fcfe" href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection">[email protected]</a></strong>.</p>
<h3 id="what-fully-handled-includes">What "fully handled" includes<a aria-label="Copy link to What " class="heading-anchor" fully="" handled"="" href="#what-fully-handled-includes" includes"="">#</a></h3>
<p>Every managed tier is built on three things working together:</p>
<ul><li><strong>AI does the volume.</strong> The same engines behind our software modules generate your blog articles, Google Business Profile posts, and social content at scale.</li><li><strong>A human manager owns quality.</strong> A dedicated manager reviews the output, fixes anything off, sets the strategy, and makes sure what goes live actually fits your business and voice.</li><li><strong>A monthly strategy call.</strong> You and your manager meet once a month to review performance and plan what is next.</li></ul>
<p>You get a read-only view of what is happening, regular reports, and a real person to reach - without writing a word or approving a single post yourself.</p>
