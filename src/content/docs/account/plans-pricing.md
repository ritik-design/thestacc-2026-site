title: Plans & Pricing Reference
---

 <p>theStacc uses module-based pricing, so you only pay for what you use. Pick one module, two, or all three, and you can mix tiers and billing cycles. This page is the full reference for every plan, price, and discount.</p>
<p>For step-by-step help managing a subscription (starting trials, upgrading, cancelling, invoices), see <a href="/docs/account/billing/">Billing &amp; Plans</a>. For the current public pricing and to start a plan, visit the <a href="https://thestacc.com/pricing">pricing page</a>.</p>
<h2 id="the-three-modules">The three modules<a aria-label="Copy link to The three modules" class="heading-anchor" href="#the-three-modules">#</a></h2>
<p>Each module is priced and billed separately:</p>
<ul><li><strong>Blog SEO</strong> (Content SEO) - AI-written, SEO-scored articles published to your site.</li><li><strong>Local SEO</strong> - Google Business Profile posts, optimization, reviews, and local rank tracking.</li><li><strong>Social Media</strong> - AI-generated posts published across your connected social platforms.</li></ul>
<p>The number in each plan name is how much content that plan generates per month (for example, "Blog SEO 30" produces 30 articles a month). That allowance refreshes at the start of every billing cycle.</p>

<p>The platform limit is how many connected social accounts a tier can post to (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X, and more). Every Social tier includes AI-generated, platform-specific content, auto-posting and queue management, a content calendar, and a multi-platform analytics dashboard.</p>
<h2 id="annual-billing-save-about-17">Annual billing - save about 17%<a aria-label="Copy link to Annual billing - save about 17%" class="heading-anchor" href="#annual-billing-save-about-17">#</a></h2>
<p>Use the <strong>Billed yearly / Billed monthly</strong> toggle on the pricing screen to switch cycles. Annual billing works out to roughly <strong>two months free</strong> versus paying monthly, which the app shows as a <strong>Save 17%</strong> badge.</p>
<p>When yearly is selected, the headline shows the full annual charge, with the per-month equivalent and the monthly price underneath - for example, a plan billed at "$X/yr ($Y/mo billed yearly)". The exact amount you will be charged is always confirmed on the secure checkout screen before you pay.</p>
<p>You can choose monthly or annual independently for each module - there is no requirement to put every module on the same cycle.</p>
<h2 id="bundle-discount-run-two-or-more-modules">Bundle discount - run two or more modules<a aria-label="Copy link to Bundle discount - run two or more modules" class="heading-anchor" href="#bundle-discount-run-two-or-more-modules">#</a></h2>
<p>When you already have one module active (paid or on trial) and add another, theStacc <strong>automatically applies a multi-module bundle discount</strong> at checkout. You do not need a coupon code - the discount is applied for you because the system sees your existing active module.</p>
<p>Running all three modules together comes out to roughly <strong>10-15% less</strong> than buying each one separately. The discount applies as long as you keep more than one module active.</p>
<h2 id="pro-the-all-in-one-plan">Pro - the all-in-one plan<a aria-label="Copy link to Pro - the all-in-one plan" class="heading-anchor" href="#pro-the-all-in-one-plan">#</a></h2>
<p><strong>Pro</strong> is a single plan that bundles all three modules into one purchase. Instead of buying Blog SEO, Local SEO, and Social Media separately, Pro unlocks every module from one subscription.</p>
<p>What Pro includes each month:</p>
<ul><li>10 Blog SEO articles</li><li>10 Social Media posts</li><li>10 Google Business Profile (Local SEO) posts</li></ul>
<p>That is one piece of content published automatically about every 3 days across each channel. The allowances refill at the start of each billing cycle.</p>
<p><strong>Pro is priced in Indian Rupees (₹):</strong></p>
<ul><li><strong>₹4,000 / month</strong>, or</li><li><strong>₹48,000 / year</strong> (₹4,000/mo, billed yearly)</li></ul>
<p>You buy Pro as a single subscription, and theStacc activates all three modules for you automatically once payment is confirmed. Pro appears on the pricing screen above the individual module cards as the <strong>All-in-one</strong> option.</p>
<h2 id="currency">Currency<a aria-label="Copy link to Currency" class="heading-anchor" href="#currency">#</a></h2>
<ul><li><strong>Individual module plans</strong> (Blog SEO, Local SEO, Social Media) are priced and billed in <strong>US Dollars ($)</strong>.</li><li><strong>Pro</strong> is priced and billed in <strong>Indian Rupees (₹)</strong>.</li></ul>
<p>At checkout, our payment processor handles the transaction and may present your local currency and any applicable taxes based on your billing country and card. The plan prices listed above are the reference amounts; the exact total for your region is always shown on the checkout screen before you confirm.</p>
<h2 id="trials">Trials<a aria-label="Copy link to Trials" class="heading-anchor" href="#trials">#</a></h2>
<p>Every individual module starts with a <strong>$1 trial that runs for 3 days</strong>, so you can see real output before committing. During the trial:</p>
<ul><li>You get full access to that module's features</li><li>Days remaining are shown on your dashboard</li><li>You can cancel anytime - no contract</li><li>You can upgrade to a paid plan whenever you are ready</li></ul>
<p>Each module has its own trial, so you can try one module at a time. When the trial ends, the plan you selected (including the tier and billing cycle you chose) begins. See <a href="/docs/account/billing/">Billing &amp; Plans</a> for how to manage or extend a trial.</p>
