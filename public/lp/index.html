FAQ

Still thinking it over?

Is this just another AI writer?

No. An AI writer only drafts content. theStacc is a full SEO content system, research, writing, optimization, checking, and publishing. The writer is one piece of a workflow designed to rank.

Will Google penalize AI-written content?

Google's guidelines focus on quality and usefulness, not how content is produced. Every article is optimized for search intent and scored before publishing.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT writes content. theStacc ranks it. We handle keyword research, SEO scoring, brand voice matching, and automated publishing.

Can theStacc help me show up in ChatGPT and AI Overviews?

Yes — that's a core part of how we write. Every article is built for Answer Engine Optimization: clear, scannable answers up top, FAQ and HowTo schema, semantic entity coverage, and citations LLMs prefer. So when buyers ask ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, or Google's AI Overviews about your category, your brand is in the answer they quote — not just your competitor's.

Can I start with just the SEO module?

Yes. Most businesses start with the SEO module for blog writing and optimization. You can add Local and Social modules anytime from the same dashboard.

What happens if I cancel?

Every article published to your site stays there. No lock-in. We also offer a full refund within your first 30 days if you're not happy.
